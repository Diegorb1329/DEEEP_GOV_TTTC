    ## **TL;DR**

This seeks funding for DAO operations through 2025 (January - December), including the Gitcoin Grants Program operations. The DAO 2025 Strategy presents an integrated approach to governance, builder engagement, and ecosystem growth, focusing on progressive decentralization and global expansion. We are requesting $808,098 from the treasury ($673,415 + 20% reserves) to execute this vision.

*Note: The Ecosystem Collective (formerly driving DAO operations and budget) dissolved mid-2024; this request represents our evolved strategic direction.*

## **Our Vision:**  
Power Collective Action. 


## **Our Mission:** 
Empower decentralized communities to sustain and innovate open-source ecosystems through progressive decentralization and governance excellence.

## Current State & Progress

The DAO has navigated significant organizational changes in 2024, focusing on stability and restructuring:

- Maintained essential operations through the Ecosystem Collective transition
- Kept core programs running on minimal resources
- Sustained Gitcoin Grants OSS satisfaction rate at +80%
- Began redefinition of Citizens Grants Program framework
- Initiated governance structure improvements
- Successfully operated within constrained budget for past 6 months

These accomplishments, while modest, provide a foundation for our more ambitious 2025 plans.

## Budget Request

The DAO remains focused on stewarding Gitcoin‚Äôs brand, community and governance in support of the overall growth and health of the Gitcoin ecosystem.

For 2025, Gitcoin DAO is requesting $808,098 from the treasury for the year of 2025. No change since the last request, as this is the first request since Ecosystem Collective was dissolved mid 2024. ## **Key Goals for 2025**


### ***Governance***



* Updates to Governance Handbook: Define core principles and decision-making frameworks to guide progressive decentralization.
* Introduce a DAO Coordinator to oversee governance, context-sharing, and operational alignment.
* Attract and support builders through improved governance and community engagement.
* Decentralize governance processes, with 50%+ community-driven decisions by year-end.
* Onboard high-caliber stewards while fostering stronger DAO-to-DAO relationships.
* Execute a 2025 events strategy targeting APAC, SEA, and LATAM to expand builder engagement.
* Execute a new governance strategy that includes engaging 5 high-context ecosystem-savvy paid delegates.


### ***Builder Engagement***

By engaging and supporting builders, we‚Äôre directly increasing adoption and activity on Allo and within our GG rounds to support Gitcoin‚Äôs North Star.



* Expand Builder Community: Onboard 500+ new builders globally, with a focus on LATAM, APAC, and SEA.
* Promote Accessible Funding: Encourage 100+ Gitcoin Community Proposals (GCPs) and Requests for Proposals (RFPs).
* Support Innovation: Enable MVP development through hackathons and bounties to showcase Allo's potential.


### ***Regional Events***



* Host 3+ Regional Events: Engage builders in LATAM, APAC, and SEA through hackathons, workshops, and community meetups.
    * *As we won‚Äôt run events within the DAO budget directly, we‚Äôll be requesting funds for events after completing research on high value areas to target and event planning.*
* Amplify Ecosystem Awareness: Ensure 70% of attendees understand Gitcoin‚Äôs broader ecosystem.
* Promote Local Innovation: Partner with local organizations to co-create impactful funding experiments.

- 50% Builder retention target
