            <p>The GitcoinDAO Governance system is a fork of COMP/UNI style governance (not a multisig).  Tokens are released from the <a href="https://etherscan.io/address/0x57a8865cfb1ecef7253c27da6b4bc3daee5be518">GTC timelock</a> via <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-dao-governance-process-v3/10358">the governance process</a> .   Iâ€™d recommending skipping to <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-dao-governance-process-v3/10358#gitcoins-gov-alpha-details-22">this part</a> of the docs which contains the answer to your question.</p>
<p>Also worth checking out the <a href="https://github.com/gitcoinco/governance-docs">governance-docs</a>  and <a href="https://gitcoin.co/blog/introducing-gtc-gitcoins-governance-token/">launch blog post</a>.</p>
