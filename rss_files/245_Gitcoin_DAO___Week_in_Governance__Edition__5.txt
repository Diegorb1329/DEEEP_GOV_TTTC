### [ü§ñ Meta-Governance](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/governance-meta/6)

* The [thread](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-dao-governance-process-v1/7860) outlining the Gitcoin DAO Governance Process v1 has sparked an active discussion with questions and improvement suggestions on various parts.

* An in-person stewards [meet-up](https://twitter.com/owocki/status/1418531108430356481) took place at EthCC! Topics including how to improve incentivization, deliberations, and accountability of the DAO Stewards were brought up. @krrisis provided notes for review.

* The [Public Goods Funding](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-funding-and-treasury-management/129) workstream has put forward an [initial draft](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/public-goods-funding-workstream-budget-request/7993) to formalize their efforts and request a governance reward budget of 45,000 GTC to be deployed over the next 3 months. Discussion is ongoing in the thread.

* @austingriffith and @owocki put forward a [budget request ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/prototyping-workstream-moonshot-budget-request/8074) for 40,000 GTC to fund the Workstream through the next 3 months. Please comment!

* @thealexyao [proposed](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-proposal-gitcoin-grantee-content-creation-storytelling/8070) a new workstream titled ‚ÄúGitcoin Grantee Content Creation + Storytelling‚Äù to ‚Äúre-engage grantees who have graduated from Gitcoin Grants and create a formal process for them to tell their stories.‚Äù.

### [üßô‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏èüßô‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è Workstream Discussion](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/workstream-discussion/9)

[Public Goods Funding / Treasury Management](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-funding/129)

* A [vote](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmY7yvzid9KM8Jn5MrAXe853wRmk5tjNSzW2DR82q8Z5RJ) to ratify the allocation of GTC from the Community Treasury towards grant recipients in the [Gitcoin builds GitcoinDAO](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-gitcoin-builds-gitcoindao-gtc-incentive-proposal/7617/12) category of Round 10 passed! A Tally vote to distribute the funds and execute on the proposal is in progress.

* @Yalor authored a new [thread](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-gtc-backed-economy/8033) regarding the community stable coin $oneGTC previously [introduced](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-onegtc-a-stablecoin-for-the-gitcoin-community/6505) by Lior. The proposal will be open for discussion for 2 weeks leading up to the next Gitcoin Stewards Call August 2nd, with a snapshot vote that will be up soon and open until August 3rd.

* The discussion on Mini Grants Community Events is ongoing with [more details](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-proposal-draft-mini-grants-community-events-discovery-of-the-quadratics-lands/7942/6) provided by the author @Sirlupinwatson.

* @DisruptionJoe provided some [insights](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-ratify-the-results-of-grants-round-10-and-formally-request-the-community-multi-sig-holders-to-payout-matching-allocations/7832/15) on the effort to decentralize grants and allow the community to run and verify the calculations for Grant Rounds payouts.

* @HelloShreyas and the team at Llama have started making progress on a unified treasury management strategy with the hope that the public goods funding workstream budget will be passed to help fund their work.

* The topic of establishing a ruleset for potential tokens being gifted the Gitcoin DAO treasury in the future got some new attention after posts by [@Sirlupinwatson](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/pre-proposal-call-for-discussion-what-should-we-do-with-dropped-tokens/7359/5) and [@php](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/pre-proposal-call-for-discussion-what-should-we-do-with-dropped-tokens/7359/7).

[Public Goods Prototyping (Moonshot Collective)](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-prototyping/130)

* @owocki and @austingriffith formally introduced the Moonshot Collective at EthCC with a presentation titled [It's all Coordination](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KpXPym_m_wA).

* The [kickoff ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-dapp-prototyping-the-moonshot-collective/130/92)[call](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/first-moonshot-collective-rapid-prototyping-workstream-call-is-tomorrow/8049) for the Workstream was a success with a large number of attendees. A recording of the call can be found [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WQERC6cVhJU), the deck can be found [here](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1diOQp2K6UZRe9sITi-2QY9VykNGarsHggSKcnAHp4bo/edit#slide=id.ge1bd4fa5a7_0_150).

* A [budget request ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/prototyping-workstream-moonshot-budget-request/8074)to fund the Workstream through the remainder of the year was put forward by Austin and Owocki.

* @austingriffith [posted](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-dapp-prototyping-the-moonshot-collective/130/93) a request for help with general ops, meetings, website maintenance and making sure things get completed.

[Decentralization Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-decentralize-gitcoin/180)

* Created the GrantRound Factory contract [here](https://github.com/dcgtc/dgrants/pull/35)
* Workstream budget proposal created and in review
* Deciding on project licensing this week
* We are having in depth discussion around how payouts will work [here
](https://github.com/dcgtc/dgrants/issues/20)

[Anti-Fraud & Collusion
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-anti-fraud-workstream-assemble/158)

* Coordinator vacation this week

* The adjustments made for sybil defense were ratified on snapshot. At this point, there has been no increase in users reporting issues with their accounts after 1366 sybil accounts deactivated from matching.
* Policy definition considerations for the platform and Ethereum ecosystem are being drafted for review & discussion by stewards to be ratified into policy prior to GR11.

* A [post](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-anti-fraud-workstream-assemble/158/74) by parobov_p goes into detail on ways to turn Gitcoin into an identity provider. The post goes on to explain that Price of forgery ‚Äúgives the ability to quality control human verification methods with market forces. It is unbiased, precise, and responsive to new exploits.‚Äù

* In progress:
  * Comms
    * Forum post to create context of ‚ÄúHighest Level of the DAO‚Äù mapping where this workstream and substreams interact.
    * Welcome page for new contributors
  * Tech
    * Web service infrastructure for human participation in ML pipeline
    * Automations for community participation in workstreams via ‚Äúworking channels‚Äù in Discord.

[Merch, Memes, Marketing Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-suggestion-mmm-merch-memes-marketing-for-public-goods-gitcoin-gitcoindao/7066)

* A visual mapping of the Gitcoin DAO Governance Process v1 is being worked on and will be published by the workstream soon.

* Header arts for the five current active Workstreams as well as steward cards that will help both stewards, delegators, and contributors navigate the DAO are in the works.

That‚Äôs it for this week in Gitcoin DAO governance, feel free join the discussions via the links and on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/gitcoin)!
