


2023: Year in Review
https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/2023-year-in-review/17330
    As the year comes to a close, it's time for our annual reflection on the incredible journey we've embarked upon together. Please join us on a trip down memory lane as we celebrate our favorite moments from 2023. For more highlights, please visit [2023.gitcoin.co](http://2023.gitcoin.co/).

**1. A Warm Welcome Back**: Firstly, we joyously welcomed back Kevin Owocki. His return has been a source of great joy and inspiration for the team and community alike.

**2. Deep Connections and Improved Communications**: This year marked the inaugural [Gitcoin Citizens ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-citizens-round-1-results/16302) round, a significant step in deepening our connections. We've made considerable strides in improving communications with our grantees and have diligently tracked feedback to enhance our processes.

**3. Rapid Innovations by Grants Stack**: Grants Stack deserves recognition for rapidly iterating and implementing key improvements that have significantly enhanced our collective experience of web3 grants.
* Launched GS publicly
* 68 rounds run on grants stack
* Won UAGP, Polygon, Fantom, Avalanche, GMX, Arbitrum, zkSync
* Multi-round checkout
* Explorer redesign and new branding
* Launched direct grants

**4. The Strength of Allo Protocol**: Allo Protocol continued its impressive growth, launching its v2 mainnet and making substantial contributions to funding public goods, especially in [Togo](https://www.impact.stream/). Additionally, the launches of SeaGrants and record sign-ups at @sherlockdefi and @buidlbox are testament to its increasing utility and popularity.
* June/July 2023: Researched, designed, and coded [Allo v2](https://github.com/allo-protocol/allo-v2)
* August 2023: Launched v2 on testnets
* September/October 2023:
  * Conducted a contest-style [audit of v2 with Sherlock](https://audits.sherlock.xyz/contests/109)
  * Launched [AlloScan](https://allo-scan-xi.vercel.app/)
* November 2023: [Launched Allo v2 on mainnets](https://www.gitcoin.co/blog/allo-protocol-v2-launches-on-mainnet) & Created the [Allo SDK](https://github.com/allo-protocol/allo-v2-sdk)
* December 2023: Built and launched [SeaGrants](https://grants-app-smoky.vercel.app/) as a test case for the Allo SDK

**5. Sybil Defense Transformation**:  Built on new infrastructure, Gitcoin Passport has been instrumental in exporting institutional knowledge to combat bots, securing various projects effortlessly.
* Created infrastructure from scratch and launched APIs
* Growth from 80k to 750k Passports
* Launch of [onchain stamps](https://www.gitcoin.co/blog/gitcoin-passport-onchain-stamps)
* $75k+ revenue
* Hired 2 data scientists
* Dramatic UX improvements & rebranding
* From 7 to 35 partners

**6. Restructuring of PGF**: The Gitcoin Grants Program (PGF) has been restructured, forming two specialized arms: the Grants Program Team (GPT) and the team dedicated to Getting Stuff Done (GSD). This restructuring has facilitated prompt payouts and efficient allocation of over $7.6M USD.
* Launch of [QED Initiative](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gcp-010-evolving-gitcoin-grants-the-q-e-d-quadratic-experimentation-and-development-program/14174/1) > [Cluster QF](https://www.gitcoin.co/blog/wtf-is-cluster-matching-qf)
* Launch of SimpleCRM
* 454 Opps & 75 Opps Closed
* Developed DAO Wide Sales Process
* [Checker](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/introducing-checker/17292) & [Round Report Cards](https://reportcards.gitcoin.co/)
* [Round Manager Training Cohort](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/announcing-the-launch-of-round-managers-training-pilot-cohort/16724) and [Quadratic Avengers](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/introducing-the-quadratic-avengers/16684).

**7. MMM Powering Brand & Communications**: The tireless efforts and creative innovations by the MMM team have significantly elevated the brand presence and outreach across communities, aligning closely with our strategic objectives and community needs.
* Built and launched 6x brands
* Launched gitcoin.co + sites for Allo, [PGN](https://publicgoods.network/), [Gitcoin Grants](https://grants.gitcoin.co/), [Grants Archive](https://archive.grants.gitcoin.co/), etc. 
* Launching Grants Stack and gaining traction
* Successful [Schelling Point](https://schellingpoint.gitcoin.co/) Sessions
* [QF NFT](https://www.gitcoin.co/blog/crypto-advocacy-gitcoin) raises $1M for matching pool
* Shift from Brand to Product-focused
* GG18 Visuals (esp moving from GIFs > videos)

We are grateful for your continued support and participation. Wishing you a wonderful holiday season!
