# We've always been Mission-first

I am very proud that Gitcoin through all of the years & all of the changes to tactics, has always been focused on the same north star.  As our momentum has grown, we've cartographied the territory more, & our hearts have opened further, it has even expanded from Open Source to other public goods.

This is a progression I see:
From Open Source Funding > Open Source Funding & Public Goods Funding > Funding Public Goods as a means to create Human Thriving

I also see a progression from 
Kevin aritculates our mission/values => Other Internet helps us articulate our mission/values with Gitcoin Holdings => GitcoinDAO articulates its mission/values through its own means.

I'd be curious how else the community would like to see this mission articulated into the future.a

<h1>
<a name="weve-always-been-mission-first-7" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#weve-always-been-mission-first-7"></a>We‚Äôve always been Mission-first</h1>
<p>I am very proud that Gitcoin through all of the years &amp; all of the changes to tactics, has always been focused on the same north star.  As our momentum has grown, we‚Äôve cartographied the territory more, &amp; our hearts have opened further, it has even expanded from Open Source to other public goods.</p>
<p>This is a progression I see:<br>
From Open Source Funding &gt; Open Source Funding &amp; Public Goods Funding &gt; Funding Public Goods as a means to create Human Thriving</p>
<p>I also see a progression from<br>
Kevin aritculates our mission/values =&gt; Other Internet helps us articulate our mission/values with Gitcoin Holdings =&gt; GitcoinDAO articulates its mission/values through its own means.</p>
<p>I‚Äôd be curious how else the community would like to see this mission articulated into the future.a</p>
