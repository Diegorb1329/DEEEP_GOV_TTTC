    # Gitcoin Governance Report

## July 2023

Find attached the Gitcoin Governance Report. This report chronicles governance activity for the month of July of 2023 and will be the final monthly report for the execution of [[GCP-007] S18 Fund Thin Governance](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gcp-007-s18-fund-thin-governance/14149). Recall the objective of this communication were to:

1. Improve governance transparency.
2. Provide a go-to platform for proposal and comment summaries.
3. Highlight a couple governance initiatives during the period
4. Illustrate GCP and non GCP efforts being worked on or evaluated.

July was the execution of the Steward Council, closing out my governance activities, and making recommendations for Governance going forward.

### Governance activity during the period:

Steward Council Elections:

We started with +60 potential nominees and narrowed that down to 10 candidates who were willing to serve on the Gitcoin Steward Council. With this cohort we have an exceptionally unique pool of web3, academic, and web2 expertise. Also, thanks to Azeem and Laura who helped other workstreams find great candidates. For additional background on the role of the Steward Council see this presentation [here](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ZZB7J6VrqIT6vZb5GWQiTsglMScQhOd38_m30jYu4Zw/edit?usp=sharing) and the full announcement, nomination, and election results here [Steward Council Elections v3 announcement & election results!](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/steward-council-elections-v3-announcement-election-results/15767)

Read the full write up on our [Gitcoin Blog](https://www.gitcoin.co/blog/steward-council-elections-v3)

**A note of thanks:**

As we start up a Steward Council v3, we need to thank our past Council members. This group of 16 people met 2-4 times per month to discuss issues, share results, and gain context as part of this ongoing experiment to provide broader input to Gitcoin Leadership. ### Looking forward ‚Äì governance recommendations:

In early July I let Kyle and Rena know that I would not be submitting a new funding request for Gitcoin Governance and that I intended to close out my time at Gitcoin with the end of S18. In preparation for this work, I built this summary of suggestions for how Gitcoin could manage Governance, I pledged my support to help transition, and reached out to several Governerds who may be interested in helping. I look forward to playing the role of a more vocal steward as I step down from my governance responsibilities.
