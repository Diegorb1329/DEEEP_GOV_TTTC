    **Treasury Diversification Working Group Update - 3/12/2022**

**Update from Last Week**
* Reached out to more DAOs, VCs, and other potential candidates to have them fill out our interest form.
* Had our first weekly working group call to sync on updates and discuss terms and partnership structure.
* Reached out to counsel to discuss legal aspects of the diversification. 
* Continued initial calls with interested strategic partners. 

**Plan for This Week**

* Process any new form submissions. 
* Continue to have initial calls with partners
* Share any conflicts of interest (as defined in our Ethics Policy in [forum post 2 ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/treasury-diversification-part-ii-welcoming-strategic-partners/9825)).
