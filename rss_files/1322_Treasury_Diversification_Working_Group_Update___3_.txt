    **Treasury Diversification Working Group Update - 3/5/2022**


**Update from Last Week**

* Reached out to more DAOs, VCs, and other potential candidates to have them fill out our interest form.
* Set up a recurring weekly call for the working group to discuss any updates on the process
* Began having calls with interested strategic partners

**Plan for This Week**

* Process any new form submissions
* Continue to have initial calls with partners
* Continue to finalize terms of the partnership (e.g. vesting schedule, etc.)
* Share any conflicts of interest (as defined in our Ethics Policy in [forum post 2](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/treasury-diversification-part-ii-welcoming-strategic-partners/9825)).

**Asks from the Community**
Our focus is still on reaching out to potential strategic partners to have initial conversations.
