## The Final Results Are In! 🚀🌱📈

The [Unicef Alpha Round](https://go.gitcoin.co/blog/gitcoin-unicef-qf-collaboration-pilot) ended yesterday, with a grand total of ~$97k in donations, from 15,507 unique donors. Thank you to all who donated, as well as those who promoted the round, and those at Gitcoin who worked to make this grants round a reality!

See the [final results here](https://dune.com/cerv1/gitcoin-unicef).

*P.S. We are aware of an issue with the grants protocol in which the Unicef Round ended [some hours earlier than expected](https://twitter.com/gitcoin/status/1605688414241636354). This is the first of five Alpha Test rounds, and we appreciate your understanding.*
