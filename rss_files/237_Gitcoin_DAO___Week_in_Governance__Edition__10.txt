* Our biggest visible accomplishment this week is the ramp-up to launch tip.party
* We‚Äôre also upgrading the tech, content, and team behind scaffold-eth
* We‚Äôre putting together a media strategy, more on this soon!
* We‚Äôre building a foundation of scalable pipelines and playbooks, the results will be invisible, but have many knock-on effects that help keep our project velocity high

[Decentralization Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-decentralize-gitcoin/180)

* The transaction from the Gitcoin DAO treasury towards funding for the Decentralization Workstream has been [executed](https://www.withtally.com/governance/gitcoin/proposal/3) on-chain.
* The first MVP of decentralized grants is close to completion to be showcased for a ‚ÄúGitcoin Building Gitcoin‚Äù side round!

](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/Fraud-Detection-Defense-f1a2969a9f324f57a02ef69a60b25cae)

* New contributors are starting to be onboarded in preparation for Grants Round 11 defense
* Blockscience continues to put out [great research](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3907693), this time on the role of algorithms in governance

[Merch, Memes, Marketing Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-suggestion-mmm-merch-memes-marketing-for-public-goods-gitcoin-gitcoindao/7066)

* The MMM Workstream onboarding experience for new contributors has seen an overhaul with a new [Typeform](https://form.typeform.com/to/YuA2wHkg). A second Typeform to [request](https://form.typeform.com/to/iM5lAFFD) memes, merch, marketing or anything between, is also live.
