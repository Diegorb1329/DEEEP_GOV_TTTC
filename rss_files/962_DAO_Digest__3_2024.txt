# üî• Hot Topics

![|720x404](upload://q9qnzEwcnysPlKvOJ9ynMlnR4zB.png)

üÜï Gitcoin is [Entering the End Game](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-entering-the-end-game/17560), shifting its focus to grants, forming a dedicated business unit, shutting down unrelated projects, and aiming to distribute $1 billion through its grant platform in 2024.

üìÆ Take the short Gitcoin Passport survey to improve its usability, scoring, features, and overall experience! [Your voice matters!](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/we-need-your-voice-help-improve-gitcoin-passport/17757)

üî∑ Gitcoin Grants is doubling down on OSS (Open Source Software) funding in the Ethereum ecosystem by improving efficiency, [focusing on high-demand areas](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-grants-proposed-updates/17649), and transitioning the community rounds to a governance-led model.

ü§ù We are accepting self-nominations for the [GG20 Community Council](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gg20-community-council/17749) for reviewing and voting on Community Rounds. Members will be selected by February 23rd via a Gitcoin DAO vote lasting 3 days. [Apply here](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/nominations-for-gg20-community-council/17750).

üëã Following Gitcoin's restructuring, the [MMM team will conclude operations by end of February](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/announcing-the-spin-down-of-mmm/17643), utilizing remaining funds of over $300,000 to cover expenses, with any surplus returned to the Gitcoin treasury, while commemorating their journey with a Memepalooza event on March 13.

üß™ The [Grants Lab](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/grants-lab-s21-budget-proposal/17636), a new Gitcoin unit focused on product development and adoption, aims to increase total grant value through Allo platform usage and prioritizes product, developer relations, marketing, and grant operations.

üéà Ecosystem Collective updates will include governance structures and budgeting, with plans for upcoming events in Brussels and ongoing marketing efforts for GG rounds.

üßê Gitcoin proposes a new "matching on matching" program offering up to $25k in additional funding for community-led grant rounds meeting specific criteria,[ subject to review and approval](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/review-gg20-eligibility-criteria-community-rounds/17846) by the DAO-elected council.

üí° Allo v2 could shift from B2B to [B2D (business-to-developer) strategy](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/allo-v2-b2c-strategy/17744) by offering pre-built, easy-to-use capital allocation contracts, creating a network effect similar to OpenZeppelin and attracting more developers, ultimately making Allo the go-to resource for this functionality.

ü§ì The Zuzalu round came with valuable insights for streamlining future processes.

üìà Passport growth is substantial, with over 100,000 new unique passports and 300,000 GTC staked in the last month.

üëÄ Integration with zkSync Guilds is driving engagement, with scores determining eligibility for Discord roles and airdrops.

üì¢ Denver plans include the Ecosystem Growth Summit with volunteer opportunities available. More can be learned on the Citizens space this week.

üöÄ Gitcoin is launching its [Citizen Grants program](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-gitcoin-s-citizen-grants-program/17470), split into Citizens Retro (Retroactive Funding), Citizens Innovate (Citizen Proposals) and Citizens Forward (RFPs), to continue fostering innovation and [community participation](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-citizens-what-are-you-doing/16922).

üÜï Grants Managers can create and run rounds on either **Base** or **zkSync Era** via [Grants Stack Manager](http://manager.gitcoin.co)! and similarly, any builders can also choose to create a project profile on those chains via [Grants Stack Builder](https://builder.gitcoin.co/).

üîö PGN will be sunset [(lights out) in June of 2024](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/announcing-the-spin-down-of-pgn-eol-june-2024/17430). In the meantime, the plan is to disable bridging onto PGN, and users are encouraged to bridge assets off PGN as they need them.

üé´ Find out how to get a FREE ticket for [ETHDenver 2024](https://www.youtube.com/@ETHDenver/videos)!

üìñ Freshen up on essential [Gitcoin Vocabulary](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/vocabulary-gitcoin-grants-programs-rounds-etc/16773).
