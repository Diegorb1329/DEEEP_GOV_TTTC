* [Kyle](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kyle/summary) provides [an update](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-the-gitcoin-foundation-a-proposal-to-represent-the-gitcoin-dao-irl-by-a-cayman-islands-foundation/9983/30) on the Foundation established to represent GitcoinDAO. The Foundation has a draft of [bylaws](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1zeSg6piVFNCsYuCSMlwUtiEgnMvHqNsi9oPO6zhlqdU/edit#) that define GTC token holders as beneficiaries of the Foundation and empowers a board of directors to execute DAO resolutions. In addition, three subsidiaries of the foundation have been formed:
  * one for holding intellectual property (trademarks, software),
  * one for operations and payments, and
  * one for Grants Multisig custody to handle KYC and AML requirements.

* [Elliot](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/elliott/summary) from Llama provides [May financial statements](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-financial-reporting/8867/35) for the DAO showing we claimed ~19M GTC from our time-locked vester account.

* [Kevin Olsen](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kevin.olsen/summary) proposes we implement [a carbon neutral policy at Gitcoin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-carbon-neutral-policy-at-gitcoin/10823) and shares a working [doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19LMbUgMb2y_xFD27gWLRnzo3KhdInZXRQWZFvVfW0vs/edit#gid=0) from the team at [KlimaDAO](https://www.klimadao.finance/). The document estimates our ‚Äútotal on-chain emissions to date, representing all trading of GTC, all donations made during our grants rounds, and our grants payouts across Mainnet, ZKSsync, and Polygon.‚Äù This amounts to 2068 tCO2e, but ‚Äúless the amount already offset and in the treasury, we have 1862 tons to purchase. If we do this with NCT our offset for Phase 1 will cost $4.52 * 1862 = $8416.24.‚Äù

* [Simona Pop](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/pop/summary) writes and shares [a reflection on DAO governance](https://pop.mirror.xyz/NbNlmtjw3hTzVHiCU9dBjcgFDxpD91UY8DOtl5Ht_x0) that quickly received [over 150 retweets](https://twitter.com/Sim_Pop/status/1533886789945114624?s=20&t=e6_JYnVpJhqwKBREq6_kKA). In the article, Simona says ‚ÄúEvolving DAOs feels like building a house on quicksand. During an earthquake. In the rain. It's all about balance. It‚Äôs an extreme exercise in balance above everything else." Simona also points out four key pillars for maintaining balance in a DAO:
  * having *standards* of information flow,
  * holding space for *engagement*,
  * seeking *equilibrium* between extremes, and
  * requesting *responsibility*.

* [Schelling Point NYC](https://twitter.com/schellingpoint_/status/1534206102379823107) is happening in just two weeks! Protocol Labs is hosting [FundingTheCommons](http://fundingthecommons.io/) and we will be running an unconference track! Don‚Äôt forget to [submit](https://gitcoin.typeform.com/SPNYC?typeform-source=t.co) your unconference topic proposal!

* [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki/summary) [shares](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/how-should-i-change-my-delegations/10828) how and why he‚Äôs delegated GTC among stewards and asks how he should change his GTC delegation in preparation for season 15.
