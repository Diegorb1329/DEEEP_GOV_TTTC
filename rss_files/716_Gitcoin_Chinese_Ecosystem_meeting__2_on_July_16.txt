# 第二次中文会议 7月16日

================English================

Attendees: Bob, Robert, 懂数据, Loner, Dreamer, etc (sorry I missed others' name)

Agenda:

1. Gitcoin information sync
2. Gitcoin knowledge base content translation progress
3. Free discussion

Action

* login Gitcoin with other identity like twitter, google etc. (not only github)
* create Chinese Quest

## Free discussion Topics

Loner - GTC discussion, (how to engage more people in Gitcoin community, not only hodler, but contributors)

Loner - how to raise a proposal

Why I cannot vote on snapshot, the reasons are:

1. Do you have GTC in your wallet?
2. Did you delegate your GTC to the stewards?
3. Do you have GTC before the block height of the snapshot voting

shawn - learn the Gitcoin DAO, besides grants, what are the Gitcoin core team doing?
