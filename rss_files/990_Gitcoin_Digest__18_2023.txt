## What‚Äôs new?

V2 documentation (WIP) is available [here](https://docs.allo.gitcoin.co/) üôÇ

Major Season 19 Initiatives: 1) V2 audit and mainnet deployment. 2) App integrations / integration tools to help builders on Allo. 3) Bootstrap the profile registry with cGrants data so that products can tap into an initial trust and verification layer.

Product Roadmap Updates ‚Äì we are ahead of schedule üí™ hot in pursuit of 3 types of partnerships:

1. Funder partners for continued growth
2. Core product integrations with the likes of BuidlBox and Impact Stream
3. Complementary product integrations

Other updates

* Developer Lead Gen Form ‚Äì Introduced a [developer lead generation form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1VbBg-CWIz-GNR-kM7WwKAmlADYKoY2cCDtgh3vxYtEk/viewform?edit_requested=true), fostering collaboration and contribution.
* Strategy Library ‚Äì Introduced a [comprehensive strategy library](https://github.com/allo-protocol/allo-v2/tree/module-library/contracts/strategies) encompassing both "Production-ready" and "Proof of Concept" strategies, fostering modular innovation.
* Marketing ‚Äì Allo brand book plus exported new assets can be found [here](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/Allo-Brand-Asset-Database-0289019192ed4c5d8832a9d9515beb07) and the [refreshed slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1yJomvIKAt_UjWDsL1EtsEjIF1hCwR4mpYbXQyIThZoI/edit?usp=sharing) have also been shipped.
* GS v2 Migration Support ‚Äì Actively aiding the migration of the Grants Stack to Allo v2, ensuring a smooth transition.

## What‚Äôs new?

Release Notes are [here](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/What-s-New-Gitcoin-Grants-Stack-8495cce451bf4bc6881539746902e7c5) üôÇ

* Fantom Wallet Integration ‚Äì In light of a recent hack and to avoid deterring users, only valueless GTC vote tokens will be used during testing phases.

* Direct Grants Integration ‚Äì Still in research mode, however, the team is exploring how to bring less crypto-native communities into the Gitcoin ecosystem. The plan is to enable the Gitcoin Grants Program and PGN engage with more ‚Äònormie‚Äô philanthropic communities.

* Notable L2 Integrations ‚Äì Deploying GS to Arbitrum, with potential integrations with other L2s coming soon

* Other Updates ‚Äì Updated Grants Stack One Pager & GeoWeb Case Study Brief in progress

## What‚Äôs new?

Checkout the new brand identity [here](https://www.figma.com/file/QvXFaL4bgPQgJuYXA9fN7b/Passport-Brand-Identity?type=design&node-id=1401-554&mode=design) üôÇ

* Onchain Passport ‚Äì Gitcoin Passport holders can now move their Passports onchain. This means that your reputation built with [@GitcoinPassport](https://twitter.com/gitcoinpassport/status/1697246879770460659?s=20) will now be able to be read natively via smart contracts in the Ethereum Ecosystem, enabling you to access high quality, trustworthy onchain opportunities over time.

* New Branding ‚Äì Redesign of the Passport branding and dashboard for improved usability and visual clarity, ensuring alignment with the mission and vision while presenting a compelling identity.

* Historical Scores Planning ‚Äì Strategically planning to capture historical scores for analysis, offering insights into Passport usage patterns over time.

* ComposeDB Upgrade Planning ‚Äì Planning an upgrade to ComposeDB to optimize performance and provide a robust foundation for future Passport developments.
