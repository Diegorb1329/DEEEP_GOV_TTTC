    ⚠️Announcement: Launch of the DeSci Quadratic Funding Round on Gitcoin

We're thrilled to announce the launch of the Decentralized Science Quadratic Funding Round on Gitcoin!

This is your chance to receive funding through Gitcoin.

🌍 Who Can Contribute?

Anyone with a passion for the future of science and exploration! Whether you're a researcher, a tech enthusiast, an artist, or simply someone who believes in the power of open knowledge, your contribution matters.

🔗 How to Get Involved:

1. Visit our application page on Gitcoin: https://explorer.gitcoin.co/#/round/42161/399
2. Create your project on https://builder.gitcoin.co
3. Apply on Arbitrum

💡 Calling All DeScientists:

Do you have a project that can revolutionize the way we think about science? Submit it for funding consideration! We are looking for projects that are not only groundbreaking in terms of scientific achievement but also embody the principles of openness, transparency, and inclusivity.

📅 Timeline:

- Application Period:
Tue, 16 Apr 2024 16:00:00 UTC - Tue, 07 May 2024 00:00:00 UTC
- Round Dates:
Tue, 23 Apr 2024 00:00:00 UTC - Tue, 07 May 2024 00:00:00 UTC

Join us in making the Decentralized Science Quadratic Funding Round a success!
