## What’s Happening in the DAO This Week

* **DAO Stewards V2 vote has passed** – The proposal to update protocols and processes for DAO Stewards passed. Folks in the comments are currently asking, “[Should we increase minimum compensation for DAO Stewards?](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-v2-of-the-steward-council/11639/22?u=alexdwagner)”
* **The Open Data Science hackathon continues through the 31st**. Check it out by going to [#start-here](https://discord.com/channels/562828676480237578/912467149015101482) in the Discord and selecting the Hackathon and Events role, then dropping into the #fdd-hackathon channel!
* **The Reserve Protocol x Gitcoin hackathon began on 10/25, and runs through 12/7**. Join and hack with others on the future of asset-backed currencies. $250k in total prizes. [Get involved here](https://gitcoin.co/hackathon/reserve-launch/onboard).
* **There is a Snapshot vote up to ratify GR16 round structure**. Read the proposal and [vote here.](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/0xecc9736dc4f9e6eb954b67a0d7301ecbcc48bbd5ad31fbd28f1e74ade500c479)

## 🐦 From Twitter

* As mentioned above, the [Reserve Protocol Hackathon](https://twitter.com/gitcoin/status/1582756881759899648?s=20&t=ny-MvR9VhGOxZL-qPIhDhw) began this week on 10/25. If you missed the Twitter Space kicking off the hackathon, [you can listen to it here](https://twitter.com/mattimost/status/1585033967606321152?s=20&t=s5CGOacOOK-HC9Iz8kZ0Nw).

* The [Elysium Hackathon](https://twitter.com/gitcoin/status/1584822015944712192?s=20&t=KMZr-Y2LUhR3IMJ7GQldRQ), also hosted on Gitcoin, runs from 11/1-11/28. Join others to build on Everscale, a fast, scalable, and fully decentralized 5th-gen blockchain. $40k in prizes. [Learn more here.
](https://gitcoin.co/hackathon/everscale/onboard)
* Lastly, [Quadratic Voting is now available on Snapshot](https://twitter.com/shawnlgrubb/status/1585296350404648960?s=20&t=qgJm-JEBVDgWxzv_KmEZmQ) 👀📈

## ☑️ From the Governance Forum

* [**Knowledge Transfer: Characterizing the Sybil Resistance Problem**](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/knowledge-transfer-characterizing-the-sybil-resistance-problem/11235), **by** [**Owocki**
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki)This post was originally posted on August 5th. Disruption Joe dropped [this link to Owocki and Kevin Olsen’s talk](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/knowledge-transfer-characterizing-the-sybil-resistance-problem/11235/13?u=alexdwagner) at DevCon Bogotá 2022, titled “1 Human, 1 Vote ➡ Money Legos”. Joe encourages devs to check out the developer focused presentation on Passport, which begins about 26 minutes into the video.

* [**Proposal: V2 of the Steward Council**](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-v2-of-the-steward-council/11639/1), **by** [**shawn16400**
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/shawn16400)This proposal to create new processes and structure for the Steward Council has gone to a [Snapshot vote and passed](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/0x3c66d92024528cdcac3d541cfaf1842d4539f4b577e9a95ff6c62285506e1f26), with 99.83% voting ‘for’! However, there is still a lively discussion in the comments.

## 🎙Latest Episodes from [Green Pill](http://availableon.com/greenpill)

* [Green Pill #56 | Democracy with Decentralized Identity with Paula Berman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DjUdJ8c8hek) – Kevin is joined by [Paula Berman](https://twitter.com/_paulaberman) who talks about cultivating web3 era democracy with decentralized identity.
