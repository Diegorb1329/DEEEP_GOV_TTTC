üöÄ Gitcoin is launching its [Citizen Grants program](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-gitcoin-s-citizen-grants-program/17470), split into Citizens Retro (Retroactive Funding), Citizens Innovate (Citizen Proposals) and Citizens Forward (RFPs), to continue fostering innovation and [community participation](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-citizens-what-are-you-doing/16922).

üß† Grantee discovery remains an unsolved problem and @rohit is proposing prototypes for solving it. Learn more about his early results and provide feedback for [Personalized Grantee Recommendations](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/concepts-pocs-and-early-results-for-personalized-grantee-recommendations/17469).

ü§ù DeveloperDAO has allocated 500k CODE tokens to Gitcoin in line with our partnership agreement. These tokens are currently held in the Safe, playing a key role in [uniting our communities](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/update-gitcoin-and-developerdao-partnership-in-2024/17423) and leveraging our combined strengths to support the growth of the web3 space.

üèÉ‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏è Can we create more value for Gitcoin by Rapid Prototyping in highest value areas? @owocki proposes [a new workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/temp-check-rapid-prototyping-gitcoin/17419) to create alignment among stakeholders that can act as a 0-1 booster/assist for 0-10 product workstreams.

üÜï Grants Managers can create and run rounds on either **Base** or **zkSync Era** via [Grants Stack Manager](http://manager.gitcoin.co)! and similarly, any builders can also choose to create a project profile on those chains via [Grants Stack Builder](https://builder.gitcoin.co/).

üîö PGN will be sunset [(lights out) in June of 2024](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/announcing-the-spin-down-of-pgn-eol-june-2024/17430). In the meantime, the plan is to disable bridging onto PGN, and users are encouraged to bridge assets off PGN as they need them.

üìä Explore [Allo Protocol multichain metrics and trends](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/allo-protocol-multichain-metrics-and-trends/17475) with data sourced from Arbitrum, Avalanche, Base, Ethereum, Fantom, Optimism, Polygon, and zkSync.
