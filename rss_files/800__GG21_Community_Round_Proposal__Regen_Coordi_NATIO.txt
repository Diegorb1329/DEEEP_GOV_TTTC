## The Regen Coordi-NATION Round

The Regen Coordi-NATION Round is a proposal for a GG21 community round centered on the themes of[ Coordi-nations](https://jessykate.medium.com/coordi-nations-a-new-institutional-structure-for-global-cooperation-3ef38d6e2cfa),[ Cosmo-localism](https://www.thealternative.org.uk/dailyalternative/2019/5/13/what-is-cosmo-localism-and-why-we-think-its-a-game-changer),[ Bio-Regionalism](https://darkmatterlabs.org/Bioregional-Financing-Facilities), and[ Regenerative Finance (ReFi)](https://www.regenerative.fi/insights/what-is-refi-regenerative-finance). Bringing together [GreenPill Network](https://greenpill.network/), [ReFi DAO](https://www.refidao.com/), [Celo Regional DAOs](https://forum.celo.org/t/regional-dao-review-template-by-luukdao/7459) and [Regens Unite](https://www.regensunite.earth/), we aim to support a global movement of regenerative communities leveraging Web3 for real-world impact and addressing local needs. The round will feed into a program to support the further development of local community impact tracking methodologies, grant tooling interoperability, and integrated QF & RPGF. We have currently secured $60K in funding from Celo Public Goods, ReFi DAO and Greenpill and ask for Gitcoin‚Äôs backing as we embark on a shared mission to showcase how community coordination, Web3, and innovative financial mechanisms can enable ecological and social regeneration on a global scale.

**Social Handles**

[@ReFiDAOist](https://x.com/ReFiDAOist)[ @greenpillnet](https://x.com/greenpillnet)[ @CeloPublicGoods](https://x.com/CeloPublicGoods)[ @regensunite](https://x.com/regensunite). Coming together as The Regen Coordi-NATION we have also created [@RegenCoordinate](https://x.com/RegenCoordinate).

### Eligibility Criteria

* We are seeking ReFi DAO Local Nodes, GreenPill Local Chapters, and Celo Regional DAOs to participate in the round. In addition, if approved by The Regen Coordi-NATION team, there is potential for other global-local regeneration networks to join the program.
* Each local community must receive a nomination from the respective evaluation and governance processes of ReFi DAO, GreenPill, or Celo Public Goods (Celo PG). This devolved approach aims to leverage each network's unique context, diversity, and expertise to provide robust application vetting.
* In addition, each must also:
  * Have a Twitter account
  * Adhere to Gitcoin‚Äôs basic rules and code of conduct, including no fraudulent activity, quid pro quo, hate speech, or other activities out of alignment with Gitcoin‚Äôs essential intents.
  * Provide an update on progress and impact if they are a grantee from previous Gitcoin rounds.
  * Include feasible milestones and a roadmap for upcoming plans and development.
* ReFi DAO, GreenPill, and Celo Public Goods (Celo PG) can also nominate up to three additional closely affiliated projects that provide supportive services to the network.
* Finally, taking an optimistic governance approach, the Regen Coordination round team and advisors will review nominated applications to provide a final level of checks.

### Funding Mechanisms & Impact Assessment Plan

In addition to leveraging the strengths of both QF & RPGF, a key outcome of this program will be supporting the development of local community impact tracking methodologies, integrated grant tooling interoperability and UX, and documentation of key results and insights.

* **QF with Gitcoin Grants Stack for GG21**: The QF mechanism brings a bottoms-up signal of the support for each of the local communities in the program, as well as raising additional funds through the crowdfunding donations. We also plan to combine this with a quadratic signal boosting mechanism.
* **Karma GAP for impact tracking and reporting**: All grantees will have their project uploaded into Karma GAP. Furthermore, bespoke development of a contextual schema, documentation guidelines, and hands on support, will facilitate detailed reporting of milestones, updates, and impact.
* **Exploration of Hypercerts**: ReFi DAO and GreenPill have both run experiments with local community impact tracking with hypercerts. From these learnings, we have developed some initial visions for how to integrate Karma GAP and Hypercerts, see ‚Ä£ [Karma GAP x Hypercerts x Regen CoordiNATION](https://www.notion.so/refi-dao/Karma-GAP-x-Hypercerts-x-ReFi-DAO-Brainstorm-fa1713f7d73543928b183d66b11d0986?pvs=4). We will continue to work with[ Mahesh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/maheshvmurthy/),[ Holke](https://www.linkedin.com/in/holke/overlay/about-this-profile/), and their respective teams to further explore these integrations.
* **Retro Funding Round with EasyRPGF.xyz**: Incentivising impact and reporting, we will follow up with a retroactive round leveraging EasyRPGF.xyz. This will include dedicated development towards advancing interoperability with Grants Stack, Karma GAP, and Hypercerts.
* **Program Retrospective**: Feedback, insights, and post-round analysis will be documented and shared publicly in Q4.

### Alignment with Gitcoin

* **Community First**: The core focus of the Regen Coordi-NATION is community empowerment, creating fundamental alignment with [Gitcoin‚Äôs Mission](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/update-gitcoins-adopted-mission-and-vision-statements/15039) to enable communities to build, fund and protect what matters.
* **Gitcoin Adoption**: Bringing multiple influential organizations together and investing in local communities introduces a powerful opportunity to enhance the network effects within the Gitcoin ecosystem. Each community and their members onboarded to Gitcoin during our rounds are likely to be part of Gitcoin for many years to come, or even leverage Gitcoin‚Äôs tools to run their own local or bio-regional rounds (as evidenced by [ReFi Phangan](https://twitter.com/PhanganQF) and others). As a result, we can help to stimulate a reinforcing cycle of adoption and engagement in Gitcoin‚Äôs products and principles, and begin to attract public, private and philanthropic sources of capital in future programs as we prove out the model.
* **Innovation & Experimentation**: The program aligns with Gitcoin‚Äôs modular and multi-mechanism approach by integrating QF, Signal Boosting, RPGF, Karma GAP, and Hypercerts. Additionally, by supporting the further development of the tooling, we aim to enhance interoperability, functionality, and contribute to the long-term adoption and effectiveness of the Web3 grant funding ecosystem.

            <p>Thank you for the detailed proposal on the Regen Coordi-NATION Round. While this current round includes diverse regions there is a strong case for having future rounds specifically focused on distinct regions like Latin America. Here are three arguments to support a regional-focused iteration:</p>
<p><strong>1. Localized Impact</strong>: Regional rounds can address specific local challenges and leverage unique opportunities within a given area. Latin America, for example, has distinct environmental, social, and economic contexts that can benefit from tailored regenerative finance (ReFi) solutions.</p>
<p><strong>2. Strengthening Regional Networks</strong>: A dedicated Latin American round would strengthen the connections between local nodes, fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing. This can create a robust network of regenerative communities that support each other‚Äôs growth and impact.</p>
<p><strong>3. Impact Tracking and Evaluation</strong>: It becomes easier to track progress and measure impact within a more homogeneous context. This allows for more accurate evaluations and the ability to tailor support mechanisms based on regional feedback.</p>
<p>To summarize, while the current Regen Coordi-NATION Round inclusively supports Latin American communities, establishing dedicated regional rounds in the future would significantly enhance the initiative‚Äôs effectiveness and impact. By doing so, we can ensure that the unique needs and opportunities of each region are fully addressed, fostering stronger, more resilient, and more engaged regenerative communities.</p>

<p>The Regen Coordi-NATION Round is a proposal for a GG21 community round centered on the themes of<a href="https://jessykate.medium.com/coordi-nations-a-new-institutional-structure-for-global-cooperation-3ef38d6e2cfa" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Coordi-nations</a>,<a href="https://www.thealternative.org.uk/dailyalternative/2019/5/13/what-is-cosmo-localism-and-why-we-think-its-a-game-changer" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Cosmo-localism</a>,<a href="https://darkmatterlabs.org/Bioregional-Financing-Facilities" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Bio-Regionalism</a>, and<a href="https://www.regenerative.fi/insights/what-is-refi-regenerative-finance" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Regenerative Finance (ReFi)</a>. Bringing together <a href="https://greenpill.network/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">GreenPill Network</a>, <a href="https://www.refidao.com/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">ReFi DAO</a>, <a href="https://forum.celo.org/t/regional-dao-review-template-by-luukdao/7459" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Celo Regional DAOs</a> and <a href="https://www.regensunite.earth/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Regens Unite</a>, we aim to support a global movement of regenerative communities leveraging Web3 for real-world impact and addressing local needs. The round will feed into a program to support the further development of local community impact tracking methodologies, grant tooling interoperability, and integrated QF &amp; RPGF. We have currently secured $60K in funding from Celo Public Goods, ReFi DAO and Greenpill and ask for Gitcoin‚Äôs backing as we embark on a shared mission to showcase how community coordination, Web3, and innovative financial mechanisms can enable ecological and social regeneration on a global scale.</p>
<p><strong>Social Handles</strong></p>
<p><a href="https://x.com/ReFiDAOist" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">@ReFiDAOist</a><a href="https://x.com/greenpillnet" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> @greenpillnet</a><a href="https://x.com/CeloPublicGoods" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> @CeloPublicGoods</a><a href="https://x.com/regensunite" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> @regensunite</a>. Coming together as The Regen Coordi-NATION we have also created <a href="https://x.com/RegenCoordinate" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">@RegenCoordinate</a>.</p>
<h3><a name="eligibility-criteria-2" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#eligibility-criteria-2"></a>Eligibility Criteria</h3>
<ul>
<li>We are seeking ReFi DAO Local Nodes, GreenPill Local Chapters, and Celo Regional DAOs to participate in the round. In addition, if approved by The Regen Coordi-NATION team, there is potential for other global-local regeneration networks to join the program.</li>
<li>Each local community must receive a nomination from the respective evaluation and governance processes of ReFi DAO, GreenPill, or Celo Public Goods (Celo PG). This devolved approach aims to leverage each network‚Äôs unique context, diversity, and expertise to provide robust application vetting.</li>
<li>In addition, each must also:
<ul>
<li>Have a Twitter account</li>
<li>Adhere to Gitcoin‚Äôs basic rules and code of conduct, including no fraudulent activity, quid pro quo, hate speech, or other activities out of alignment with Gitcoin‚Äôs essential intents.</li>
<li>Provide an update on progress and impact if they are a grantee from previous Gitcoin rounds.</li>
<li>Include feasible milestones and a roadmap for upcoming plans and development.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>ReFi DAO, GreenPill, and Celo Public Goods (Celo PG) can also nominate up to three additional closely affiliated projects that provide supportive services to the network.</li>
<li>Finally, taking an optimistic governance approach, the Regen Coordination round team and advisors will review nominated applications to provide a final level of checks.</li>
</ul>

<h3><a name="funding-mechanisms-impact-assessment-plan-6" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#funding-mechanisms-impact-assessment-plan-6"></a>Funding Mechanisms &amp; Impact Assessment Plan</h3>
<p>In addition to leveraging the strengths of both QF &amp; RPGF, a key outcome of this program will be supporting the development of local community impact tracking methodologies, integrated grant tooling interoperability and UX, and documentation of key results and insights.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>QF with Gitcoin Grants Stack for GG21</strong>: The QF mechanism brings a bottoms-up signal of the support for each of the local communities in the program, as well as raising additional funds through the crowdfunding donations. We also plan to combine this with a quadratic signal boosting mechanism.</li>
<li><strong>Karma GAP for impact tracking and reporting</strong>: All grantees will have their project uploaded into Karma GAP. Furthermore, bespoke development of a contextual schema, documentation guidelines, and hands on support, will facilitate detailed reporting of milestones, updates, and impact.</li>
<li><strong>Exploration of Hypercerts</strong>: ReFi DAO and GreenPill have both run experiments with local community impact tracking with hypercerts. From these learnings, we have developed some initial visions for how to integrate Karma GAP and Hypercerts, see ‚Ä£ <a href="https://www.notion.so/refi-dao/Karma-GAP-x-Hypercerts-x-ReFi-DAO-Brainstorm-fa1713f7d73543928b183d66b11d0986?pvs=4" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">Karma GAP x Hypercerts x Regen CoordiNATION</a>. We will continue to work with<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/maheshvmurthy/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Mahesh</a>,<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/holke/overlay/about-this-profile/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc"> Holke</a>, and their respective teams to further explore these integrations.</li>
<li><strong>Retro Funding Round with EasyRPGF.xyz</strong>: Incentivising impact and reporting, we will follow up with a retroactive round leveraging EasyRPGF.xyz. This will include dedicated development towards advancing interoperability with Grants Stack, Karma GAP, and Hypercerts.</li>
<li><strong>Program Retrospective</strong>: Feedback, insights, and post-round analysis will be documented and shared publicly in Q4.</li>
</ul>
