    **Voter Guides on the rise**

Just as in [Q1/S13](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/summary-s13-budget-proposal-recap-kris-s-voter-guide/9895) I created a voter guide. **I‚Äôd invite all Stewards to create guides** as a way to show accountability on voting, plus also to inform their peers, as a way to increase intersubjective [consensus](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-voter-guide-template/10488). You can start with the template created by @owocki [here](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-voter-guide-template/10488) or you can duplicate this post and make it your own. As a reader, **know that you can and should hold your steward(s) accountable** on fairly representing you, you can find your (future) Steward [via the Steward Health Cards, where you can (re)delegate your voting power at any time](https://www.daostewards.xyz/).

I hesitated to create this because every workstream who worked massively hard on this deserves a way longer explanation than the below, but because so much is at stake (bear market, overall direction of the DAO, and because perfect is the enemy of good (enough)) I went ahead: below some **holistic thoughts on how I'll be voting** plus some details per workstream in the table.

**Background: DAO Operations**

On the note of lacking time, this is due to working more than full time in the DAO Ops workstream, so I'm looking forward to dividing the workload over some more talented contributors in the near future, and slowly getting there :) We're currently looking for a [community management lead](https://angel.co/company/gitcoin/jobs/2169336-community-management-lead-gitcoin-dao) and [a support lead](https://angel.co/company/gitcoin/jobs/2095095-user-support-lead-gitcoin-dao), a treasury management job description is in the making but feel free to already reach out, and [help us](https://twitter.com/GitcoinDAO/status/1521575840080879617) [spread the word](https://twitter.com/GitcoinDAO/status/1511043414317584384?s=20&t=h1fGamBKQPQcxeF9K_QD_g). Other ways to support us is [by commenting on our workstream proposal](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-proposal-dao-operations-s14-budget-request/10447?u=krrisis) and if you are a Steward we hope you'll vote YES.

I want to note that although my comments are pretty concise they are deeply informed. Know that as an active contributor and co-workstream lead in DAO Ops I am - together with @Jodi - basically part of all the cross-workstream calls and with our various initiatives & its leads we cover a lot of ground within the DAO ([Accounting, Community Experience, Community Governance, Devops, People Ops & User Support](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-proposal-dao-operations-s14-budget-request/10447/2)). It gives a good view on how this organism, this [‚Äòslime mold‚Äô](https://twitter.com/owocki/status/1456368527347044355) :) is evolving and how we can make it better, together. There‚Äôs a lot to do.

**Gitcoin's Purpose and Essential Intent**

Especially helpful are our weekly CSDO calls (Cross Stream DAO Operations) which are part of the community experience initiative (in DAO Ops) and the extra strategy sessions (facilitated by @samspurlin from [The Ready](https://theready.com/)), which we‚Äôve organized in preparation for S14, where we dove into objectives per workstream. Our latest session, which was about GitcoinDAO‚Äôs Purpose and Essential Intent, was the session that I was waiting for to make my own decisions as a Steward. [The document you can find here](https://app.mural.co/t/theready7827/m/theready7827/1651759127362/e725e0a698f03944495507ed206cf5f90bd07428?sender=sam3202) is not our final output - next steps are being worked on by a core team - but we did reach alignment on the fact that **we want to focus on 4 points as a DAO** (random order!):

* Improve Coordination Intra Gitcoin DAO
* Gitcoin Grants 2.0 (Protocol)
* Grow Grants GMV
* Financial Sustainability

The first is at the core of what DAO Ops is about, 2 will be the key mission of the new Grants 2.0 workstream (S15) and part of what Moonshot Collective will be doing in the next seasons. Number 3 is key for Public Goods Funding and FDD (when it comes to anti-sybil defense), and 4 is spread over DAO Ops, future Grants 2.0 and MMM in my opinion, all in their own way. Logically a living organism as Gitcoin DAO does a lot more that indirectly ties into this but **the above points plus my logic derived from this will heavily influence my voting behavior.**

            <p><strong>Voter Guides on the rise</strong></p>
<p>Just as in <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/summary-s13-budget-proposal-recap-kris-s-voter-guide/9895">Q1/S13</a> I created a voter guide. <strong>I‚Äôd invite all Stewards to create guides</strong> as a way to show accountability on voting, plus also to inform their peers, as a way to increase intersubjective <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-voter-guide-template/10488">consensus</a>. You can start with the template created by <a class="mention" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki">@owocki</a> <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-voter-guide-template/10488">here</a> or you can duplicate this post and make it your own. As a reader, <strong>know that you can and should hold your steward(s) accountable</strong> on fairly representing you, you can find your (future) Steward <a href="https://www.daostewards.xyz/">via the Steward Health Cards, where you can (re)delegate your voting power at any time</a>.</p>
<p>I hesitated to create this because every workstream who worked massively hard on this deserves a way longer explanation than the below, but because so much is at stake (bear market, overall direction of the DAO, and because perfect is the enemy of good (enough)) I went ahead: below some <strong>holistic thoughts on how I‚Äôll be voting</strong> plus some details per workstream in the table.</p>
<p><strong>Background: DAO Operations</strong></p>
<p>On the note of lacking time, this is due to working more than full time in the DAO Ops workstream, so I‚Äôm looking forward to dividing the workload over some more talented contributors in the near future, and slowly getting there <img src="https://emoji.discourse-cdn.com/twitter/slight_smile.png?v=12" title=":slight_smile:" class="emoji" alt=":slight_smile:" loading="lazy" width="20" height="20"> We‚Äôre currently looking for a <a href="https://angel.co/company/gitcoin/jobs/2169336-community-management-lead-gitcoin-dao">community management lead</a> and <a href="https://angel.co/company/gitcoin/jobs/2095095-user-support-lead-gitcoin-dao">a support lead</a>, a treasury management job description is in the making but feel free to already reach out, and <a href="https://twitter.com/GitcoinDAO/status/1521575840080879617">help us</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/GitcoinDAO/status/1511043414317584384?s=20&amp;t=h1fGamBKQPQcxeF9K_QD_g">spread the word</a>. Other ways to support us is <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s14-proposal-dao-operations-s14-budget-request/10447">by commenting on our workstream proposal</a> and if you are a Steward we hope you‚Äôll vote YES.</p>
<p>I want to note that although my comments are pretty concise they are deeply informed. <p><strong>Gitcoin‚Äôs Purpose and Essential Intent</strong></p>
<p>Especially helpful are our weekly CSDO calls (Cross Stream DAO Operations) which are part of the community experience initiative (in DAO Ops) and the extra strategy sessions (facilitated by <a class="mention" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/samspurlin">@samspurlin</a> from <a href="https://theready.com/">The Ready</a>), which we‚Äôve organized in preparation for S14, where we dove into objectives per workstream. Our latest session, which was about GitcoinDAO‚Äôs Purpose and Essential Intent, was the session that I was waiting for to make my own decisions as a Steward. <a href="https://app.mural.co/t/theready7827/m/theready7827/1651759127362/e725e0a698f03944495507ed206cf5f90bd07428?sender=sam3202">The document you can find here</a> is not our final output - next steps are being worked on by a core team - but we did reach alignment on the fact that <strong>we want to focus on 4 points as a DAO</strong> (random order!):</p>
<ul>
<li>Improve Coordination Intra Gitcoin DAO</li>
<li>Gitcoin Grants 2.0 (Protocol)</li>
<li>Grow Grants GMV</li>
<li>Financial Sustainability</li>
</ul>
<p>The first is at the core of what DAO Ops is about, 2 will be the key mission of the new Grants 2.0 workstream (S15) and part of what Moonshot Collective will be doing in the next seasons. Number 3 is key for Public Goods Funding and FDD (when it comes to anti-sybil defense), and 4 is spread over DAO Ops, future Grants 2.0 and MMM in my opinion, all in their own way. Logically a living organism as Gitcoin DAO does a lot more that indirectly ties into this but <strong>the above points plus my logic derived from this will heavily influence my voting behavior.</strong></p>
