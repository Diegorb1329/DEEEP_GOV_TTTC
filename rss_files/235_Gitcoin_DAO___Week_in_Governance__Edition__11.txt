* The second [Steward Unconference Chat](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-stewards-unconference-chats/8112) this week by @Anett. The goal of these calls is to ‚Äúmeet stewards & friends and have non formal chats together, share memes and chat about whatever we want.‚Äù

* @codingsh authored a [thread](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/dao-focused-on-our-mental-health/8515) to discuss the formation of a DAO focused on our mental health.

* Contract audit is in progress and we will have results by the end of this week
* Curation game hackathon entry has been completed and we will be picking winners by the end of next week
* Documentation project is kicking off now and will result in a base level of documentation and explainers for dGrants
