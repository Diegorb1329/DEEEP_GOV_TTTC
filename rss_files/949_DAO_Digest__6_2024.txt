‚úÖ GG20 has successfully concluded with significant improvements from GG19, excellent operational feedback and overwhelming support from the community.

üÜï GG20 introduced an external council for community grant review and governance process who [provided detailed insights and valuable feedback](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gg20-community-round-governance-a-retrospective/18766) (avg. satisfaction rating = 4.71/5) for future iterations.

üìÜ When initially elected, the Community Council members were elected to select the Community Rounds only for GG20. [Should this proposal pass](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-extending-the-term-of-gg-community-council/18776), we would extend that term beyond GG20 until December 31, 2024.

ü§† Gitcoin Citizens Retro #3 concluded with $55K crowdfunded, 50K GTC in matching and extra matching from @ThankArbitrum, bringing it to a total of $140K, making this the most successful round to date.

.üîÑ [Read this brief overview](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-citizens-rounds-value-generation-opportunities/18727) of how Gitcoin Citizens rounds contribute to increasing Allo GMV through strategic value loops that compound over time.

üåü Our primary objective for 2024 is to triple our Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) from 2023, aiming for $15 million, with a Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG) of reaching $50 million, @meglister outlines in [this post](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/2024-gmv-strategy-overview-grants-lab/18805).

üî¶ @kyle follows the [passing of the torch tradition](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/another-passing-of-the-torch/18704), departing from official Gitcoin interactions and moving into the Passport business unit to co-lead the spin-out.

ü§î Leveraging our technology and community levers will optimize growth and catalyze meaningful change. After all, Gitcoin [has always](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/harmonizing-community-and-technology-gitcoin/18799) been about advancing the common good through public goods funding.

üåá Public Goods Network [(PGN) is shutting down](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/pgn-shutdown-a-recap/18794). Despite calls for a new management plan, no viable long term options emerged. Still have unclaimed assets on the network? [Join the discussion](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/public-goods-network-retrospective-recommendation-discussion/18772).

## What‚Äôs new?

  * Explore opportunities for DAO-to-DAO collaborations
  * Facilitate strategic partnerships and collaborative ventures with other DAOs to amplify Gitcoin‚Äôs influence and drive adoption of Gitcoin‚Äôs products within the broader Web3 ecosystem
  * Leverage Gitcoin‚Äôs governance token allocations to engage in Metagovernance efforts
