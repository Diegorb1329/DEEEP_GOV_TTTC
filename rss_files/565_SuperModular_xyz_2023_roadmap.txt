            <p><a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/supermodular-2023-roadmap-tldr/237">(x-post on community.supermodular.xyz)</a></p>
<p>Hi everyone from <a href="https://supermodular.xyz/">SuperModular</a>.</p>
<p>Here is a short update on what we‚Äôre up to in 2023.</p>
<h1>
<a name="our-purpose-1" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#our-purpose-1"></a>Our Purpose</h1>
<p>Everything we do at Supermodular is grounded in our purpose.  Our purpose is to cultivate a more regenerative digital frontier.</p>
<h1>
<a name="our-roadmap-2" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#our-roadmap-2"></a>Our Roadmap</h1>
<p>We just unveiled our <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/2023-roadmap-early-preview-supermodular-incubation-program-sips/106">2023 SuperModular Roadmap Preview</a>.</p>
<p>Our roadmap is T shaped.  Part of it goes BROAD on building a movement. Part of it goes DEEP on building software.</p>
<h2>
<a name="broad-movement-building-3" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#broad-movement-building-3"></a>Broad - movement building</h2>
<p>How do we create a movement for regenerative crypto?  How do we educate people and route them to regenerative crypto projects?  How do we <a href="https://www.playbigger.com/media/how-to-be-a-category-king">design a category</a> and show off category kings like SuperModular &amp; it‚Äôs alumni (like Gitcoin and KERNEL)?  How do we <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/the-regen-movement-needs-live-players/160">act like live players until a formula to achieve our purpose appears</a>?</p>
<ol>
<li>We continue to do <a href="https://greenpill.party/">Greenpill podcasts</a>.  This recent one on the <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/greenpill-episode-drop-gitcoin-alpha-round-janine-leger-connor-oday-and-nate-gosselin/234">Grants Alpha Round on Greenpill 1/17</a> may interest you!</li>
<li>We <a href="https://owocki.com/making-pluralism-practical-%f0%9f%8c%88/">continue to do public speaking</a>.  This recent talk at Plurality Summit heavily features Gitcoin Grants, so it might interest the DAO!</li>
<li>We just signed a LOI to help co-produce Schelling Point Denver 2023.</li>
<li>We are building a <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/sip-cohort-2-opportunity-1-regen-web3-educational-research-funnel/81">soup to nuts educational funnel for from new to web3 to regen web3</a>.</li>
<li>We are experimening with other new ways of making regen crypto a movement.  This might mean a greenpill course or audiobook, or supporting local greenpill meetups across the world. <a href="https://gov.gitcoin.co">More here</a>.</li>
<li>We will have lots of fun new supermodular swag at ETHDenver.  Looking forward to attending schelling point and giving you all some!</li>
</ol>
<h2>
<a name="deep-on-building-software-4" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#deep-on-building-software-4"></a>DEEP on building software</h2>
<ol>
<li>We are launching <a href="https://buidlbox.io/">https://buidlbox.io/</a> at ETHDenver. (Buidlbox used to be known as Gitcoin Hackathons)</li>
<li>We are working with Protocol Labs on <a href="http://hypercerts.xyz/">http://hypercerts.xyz/</a>, which is launching on top of Grants Stack on ETHDenver.</li>
<li>We are building on top of grants stack.
<ol>
<li>Much of this work is <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/new-form-factors-for-quadratic-funding/165">new form factors for Quadratic Funding</a>.
<ol>
<li>
<a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/sip-cohort-2-opportunity-2-quadratic-funding-social-network/94">Quadratic Funding Social Network</a> in Lenster</li>
<li>
<a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/sip-cohort-2-opportunity-2-quadratic-funding-social-network/94">Quadratic Funding Social Network</a> in Discord</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Some of the work is just taking QF and putting it in new places.
<ol>
<li>We‚Äôre building <a href="https://community.supermodular.xyz/t/sip-cohort-2-opportunity-3-web2-5-simplegrants-platform/95">Simple Grants</a>, a web2/fiat grants tool.  Once this ships, we have leads to run rounds for (1) the democratic party (2) a town in Africa, also the state of Colorado (3) web2 Open Source.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Some other work is extending Grants Stack.
<ol>
<li>A QV fork of Allocation Protocol.</li>
<li>A tool for sorting/contacting winners of a Grants Round</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>
<a name="things-id-like-to-build-in-2023-5" class="anchor" href="https://gov.gitcoin.co#things-id-like-to-build-in-2023-5"></a>Things I‚Äôd LIKE to build in 2023.</h2>
<p>This is work that we‚Äôve not yet gotten traction on yet but would like to.</p>
<ol>
<li>a L2/block builder/block proposer/ MEV tool that funds public goods with its fee generation</li>
<li>web3 SAAS tools (long live EIP 1337) that give DAOS easy tools to generate subscription revenue</li>
<li>Map the metacrisis at <a href="https://www.metacrisisdao.com/">https://www.metacrisisdao.com/</a>
</li>
<li>We‚Äôd love to build on top of Passport.  But we‚Äôre not sure what/where/how to do that yet.</li>
<li>A product hunt for web3.  What if we built this on top of Grants Stack and/or used the Grants Stack Data to surface the best new projects?</li>
<li>One area of BIG opportunity we see is to fork Allocation Protocol and build <a href="https://medium.com/giveth/coordination-mechanisms-notes-from-a-conversation-with-griff-green-kevin-owocki-af95f3e831b1">many different coordination mechanisms</a> on top of it.  Taking Gitcoin Grants Stack from QF =&gt; a pluralism of practical coordination mechanisms.  We could do a hyperparameter sweep of whats possible in web3 human coordination and achieve MAXIMUM COORDINATION!!!</li>
<li>QF for communities I‚Äôm in:  stuff like moonshot bots, SACKS, boulder blockchain</li>
</ol>
