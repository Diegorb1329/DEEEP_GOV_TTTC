# üî• Hot Topics (Ecosystem Headlines):



**Matching results from GG18 are live! ‚Äì GG19 Opens NOV 15th**

* This round achieved a new high with $680k crowdfunded from 328k contributions, showing a 12% increase from the Beta round and a 65% increase from the Alpha round. ‚Äì making it the biggest ever on the new decentralized tech stack. Check out the detailed updates posted by @umarkhaneth

**A way to reduce the governance burden through AI digests of proposals?**

* One common pattern in DAOs is that they are impacted by excessive governance burden, which comes from the need to moderate online forums (like this one), consume proposals easily (getting to the point), differentiating proposals (the good from the bad). Would you like Gitcoin to implement the [visual solution for governance burden](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/rfc-ai-dashboard-for-governance-proposals/16561) that @skyfoxx is proposing?

**Governing Allo v2**

* The second version of Allo will be going live on main net soon. When it does, the Gitcoin community will have a new set of responsibilities: governing a protocol. This has been a long time in the making and @0xZakk is excited to share what this means for our community, check out his latest [Overview of new governance responsibilities and opportunities for GTC holders.](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/governing-allo-v2-overview-of-new-governance-responsibilities-and-opportunities-for-gtc-holders/16591)

**Allo S19 Progress Update**

* We are rapidly approaching the next budgeting cycle, take a moment to catch up on what the Allo team has been up to this season. @nategosselin has shared a comprehensive overview on key milestones and Goal Scoring, hoping that it can be a useful reference for community members as you review our upcoming S20 budget.

**Better Impact Funding: Gitcoin ü§ù Green Pill Network**

* @owocki proposes an MVP of ‚Äú[Better Impact Funding](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/better-impact-funding-the-greenpill-hypercerts-gitcoin-combo-move/16477)‚Äù enabled by integrating multiple regenerative communities, describing how to achieve Greenpill.network & Gitcoin-alignment through HyperCerts based impact funding and Gitcoin QF Street Teams üå±

**Embracing decentralized integrity: from Founder to Contributor in a new cycle**
