### **üå± [Public Goods Funding](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-funding/129)**

* Over $700k has been raised so far for Grants Round 12! Partnership discussions with other web3 x social impact organizations are underway and are planned to wrap up in the next couple weeks.

### [üöÄ Moonshot Collective](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-prototyping-workstream-moonshot-budget-request-q4-2021/8785)

* 4 of 5 ETHBot Statues have been sold, as well as 161 ETHBot NFTs in [The Greatest LARP](https://greatestlarp.com). The next level will be unlocked once 200 ETHBot NFTs have been minted. More than $200,000 has been raised to fund public goods so far. [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) introduced, on behalf of [Colton](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/colton), ‚ÄúWomp Womp V2‚Äù with [a poll](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/a-governance-question-for-greatestlarp-com/8848/25) to determine if Womp Womp should be used every 24 hours, regardless of sales. Kevin will post in [this telegram channel](https://t.me/joinchat/ekBANXnlwcQ1NzRh) when the txns are submitted. A Womp Womp is a mint cost reduction in order to help spur more participants.

* The Moonshot Collective monthly demo call was held on Monday, November 1 at 1pm. Moonshot projects have 5 minutes to demo their tech. In case you couldn‚Äôt make it a link to the videos will be included in next week‚Äôs digest.

- Every Friday the Moonshot Collective gets together to show off their progress. Individual projects demo what they‚Äôve been working on. This week three projects shared their work: Anon Vs Moloch, Mintler, Tip Party, and Decentralized Recruiter. Demo videos for each project can be found on [this YouTube playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLVupIN3GgLtjS-vgOpe5hXoZlKk5k5L0q). Check out [@MoonshotCollect](https://twitter.com/MoonshotCollect) on Twitter for frequent updates and learn more by saying[ hello in Discord](https://gitcoin.co/discord), or checking out our[ site](https://moonshotcollective.space/).

### üåê [Decentralize Gitcoin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-decentralize-gitcoin/180)

* The vote to ratify the [dGTC Workstream Budget Proposal](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/0xd65bb1c535d0c83ec197df8d000464c6fb7a6ef000ad59df48af82d39d23e42e) for Q4 2021 has passed and steps have been taken to propose a formal on-chain vote.

* The [Moonshot Collective round of dGrants](https://grants.gtcdao.net/#/dgrants/rounds/0x6c7B74D7640f401271208186e5CbBc6e7E2C73F4/grants) has been completed. The round had 11 grants, 145 contributors and $5,477 worth of matching funds available.

* Planning and preparations for running a dGrants round in the upcoming Grant Round 12 in December are underway. The workstream is working on shipping new features, performance fixes and UX improvements until then.

### üíô [Fraud Detection & Defense (FDD)](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/Fraud-Detection-Defense-f1a2969a9f324f57a02ef69a60b25cae)

* [Request to fund FDD workstream Q4 2021 budget](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/0x265fe70790524875caacb6675ef6d10b4d4ceac8b1c26df26ad1ba284eab70a1) passed quorum on Snapshot. After the deadline has been passed steps will be taken to propose a formal on-chain vote.

* Payouts for [matching pool for GR11](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmQTMLG8qbDpTCCUwVnbEmzLkdCvf1ApZ8w9nd9idfC8r7) have begun as reported by [Gitcoin Holdings & the FDD workstream](https://gitcoin.co/blog/gitcoin-grants-round-11-governance-brief/). If you‚Äôre the owner of a Grant; check your email inbox!

* All expected Q4 2021 deliverables from the FDD-worksteam are now listed on the [FDD Notion](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/Fraud-Detection-Defense-f1a2969a9f324f57a02ef69a60b25cae).

* The workstream spun out FDD Legal & FDD Accounting to the CrossStream DAOops. A process to spin out User Support in Q1 2022 is currently being planned.

* Anonymized contributor feedback mechanism for the DAO as a whole is being worked on to ensure the entire DAO can give feedback on these things rather than only stream leads.

* Fresh merch dropped Monday in the [Schwag Store](http://store.gitcoin.co/)! New items are a Public Goods embroidered black [hoodie](https://store.gitcoin.co/products/%E2%AD%90%EF%B8%8F-public-goods-hoodie) and sunny [gm mug](https://store.gitcoin.co/products/%F0%9F%8C%9E-gm-enamel-mug?_pos=&utm_medium=gmMug). * Outlines of the Meme-Museum are now underway. The Meme-Museum aims to celebrate great memes and Memelords & will be a web3-friendly archive that allows users to upload, search and reuse memes created in the broader web3-community.
