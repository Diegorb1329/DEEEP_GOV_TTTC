## [ü§ñ Meta-Governance](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/governance-meta/6)

* [A visual history of Gitcoin‚Äôs mission (2017 - 2022)](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/a-visual-history-of-gitcoins-mission-2017-2022/10049) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) is a visual overview of Gitcoin‚Äôs journey so far. ‚ÄúFrom Open Source Funding > Open Source Funding & Public Goods Funding > Funding Public Goods as a means to create Human Thriving‚Äù

* [How Soulbound Tokens Can Make Gitcoin Grants More Pluralistic](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/how-soulbound-tokens-can-make-gitcoin-grants-more-pluralistic/10077) by [Leon](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/leone) introduces the Plurality paradigm for funding public goods and details its application to Quadratic Funding and Gitcoin. The proposed social incentive design combines soulbound attestations and pairwise quadratic funding to capture cooperation between grant contributors and increase Plurality.

* [Abundance & Scarcity](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/abundance-scarcity/10045) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) raises questions regarding how we should make the most of our current abundance prior to the next potential bear market. ‚ÄùHow do we maximize the utility & anti-fragility of the Gitcoin network?‚Äù

* [GitcoinDAO as a generator function for innovation](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoindao-as-a-generator-function-for-innovation/10078) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) describes GitcoinDAO as a ‚Äúgenerator function for new coordination - protocols, DAOs, and public goods - GitcoinDAO generates net-new coordination using Open Source technologies. We create value before we capture value. We build ecosystems.‚Äù

* [The Donut Economy x 8 Forms of Capital](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/the-donut-economy-x-8-forms-of-capital/10079) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) talks about the ‚Äúworld I want to see created by regen web3, using two frameworks which I discovered during research into GREENPILLED - how crypto can regenerative the world.‚Äù ‚ÄúIf[ Gitcoin is a generator function of innovation](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoindao-as-a-generator-function-for-innovation/10078), this post is a taxonomy of what I think the DAO could point its innovation machine at.‚Äù

* [Our Flagship Product is GitcoinDAO](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/our-flagship-product-is-gitcoindao/10081) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) argues that our flagship product is actually GitcoinDAO, as opposed to Gitcoin Grants as ‚ÄúGitcoinDAO is the totality of what we do and how we show up. Gitcoin Grants is just one part.‚Äù

* [Data Science @ GitcoinDAO](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/data-science-gitcoindao/10088) by [Owocki](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/owocki) raises the question ‚ÄúWhat should the data science practice at Gitcoin look like?‚Äù The thread describes four areas of interest; Product Analytics & Data Science, Marketing Analytics & Data Science, Complex Systems Insights and Fraud insights.

* [Dan](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/schlabach) provided [an update](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/treasury-diversification-working-group-update-3-12-2022/10046) on the Treasury Diversification Working Group. If you‚Äôd like to recommend a strategic partner, please leave a comment on the [second forum post](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/treasury-diversification-part-ii-welcoming-strategic-partners/9825) or contact a member of the working group.

* [Gitcoin Appeals Discussion and Planning](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-appeals-discussion-and-planning/10035) by [David](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/David_Dyor) is a plan to ‚Äúfacilitate informed steward debate & vote for denied grant applicants seeking an appeal (...) specifically, the work-flows and automation associated with the system.‚Äù

* [Proposed Revision to Grant Eligibility Criteria](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposed-revision-to-grant-eligibility-criteria/10098) by [David](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/David_Dyor) is a proposal to ‚Äúsuggests a policy change to GitcoinDAO grant eligibility criteria. This policy change suggestion arose from the BrightID appeal request - Grant 191. This policy change is recommended by the Fraud Detection & Defence workstream of the GitcoinDAO.‚Äù

### üå± [Public Goods Funding](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Public-Goods-4ccd579f433c4dff9d8952314468d6e8)

* [Grants Round 13](http://gitcoin.co/grants) is in full swing! It‚Äôs a great time to be a grantee and contributor -- more matching funds are available than last round -- we have over $3M in matching funds up for grabs!

* Public Goods are Good! Our partners think so too. We‚Äôre partnering with:
  * [Crypto, Culture, and Society](https://cryptosociety.notion.site/cryptosociety/Crypto-Culture-Society-01f39bcff26b422183061e6011b16892) (a learning DAO studying the impact of crypto on culture and society) are kicking off [Season Two](https://twitter.com/CryptoSocietyS1/status/1489709838301081613)! Gitcoin will be playing a key role in helping build a liberal arts education for crypto

  * [DeveloperDAO](https://twitter.com/developer_dao) is bringing tons of amazing coders into the space to help us build new types of public goods
  * [DreamDAO](https://twitter.com/dreamdao_) is continuing to help push forward the solarpunk + regen narrative to show the optimistic futures we can create together
  * [Verses](https://verses.xyz) (a collective of writers, researchers, technologists) who have some of the best writing in the ecosystem about what it means to be a pluriverse.

### [üöÄ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-prototyping-workstream-moonshot-budget-request-q4-2021/8785) [Moonshot Collective](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Moonshot-Collective-ffa78f44a0de412cb607f9b69d3a376f)

* The Moonshot Collective [Season 13 budget request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/moonshot-collective-workstream-budget-request-q2-2022/9824) has passed [on chain](https://www.withtally.com/governance/eip155:1:0xDbD27635A534A3d3169Ef0498beB56Fb9c937489/proposal/20) as is queued for execution.

* Green Pill Podcast Episode 4 hosted by [Owocki](https://twitter.com/owocki) is live on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/0l6aXWC94dd0RA3tkKfxjd), [Apple](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/2-ethers-phoenix-with-optimisms-karl-floersch/id1609313639?i=1000552104369), and wherever you like to listen to podcasts. This episode features a panel of workstream leads from GitcoinDAO. They talk about Grants Round 13, which is ongoing now through March 24th.

### üíô [Fraud Detection & Defense (FDD)](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Fraud-Detection-Defense-2bde13c0b8e74fda81435d94e49e2703)

* The FDD [Season 13 budget request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-fdd-workstream-season-13-budget-request/9826/25) has passed [on chain](https://www.withtally.com/governance/eip155:1:0xDbD27635A534A3d3169Ef0498beB56Fb9c937489/proposal/23) as is queued for execution.

* Sybil Defenders
  * Our Anti-Sybil Operational Process (ASOP) has kicked off strong! We are moving this process into the DAO and will own the end-to-end process to identify and sanction sybil accounts.

  * We are running a human evaluation layer and believe more human evaluations can be done at a higher quality for lower cost!
* Grants Investigation Agency
  * Last day to get a new grant approved for GR13 is 3/15! [Apply now](http://gitcoin.co/grants) if you‚Äôve been waiting! (Shout out to [Zer8](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/zer8/summary?utm_source=gitcoindao&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=news15), [David](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/David_Dyor?utm_source=gitcoindao&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=news15), and the grants review squads for staying on top of the incoming approvals!

  * [Updated](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-appeals-discussion-and-planning/10035) the appeals process for grants which were denied to have clearer strategies and outcomes. All appeals for GR13 so far have been heard
* Ground Control
  * Our source council has gone through the first round of voting covering topics like multisig key holder responsibilities, workstream objectives and key results, and downside risk insurance.

  * Our data analytics squad is looking for requests. As we and others write code to analyze grants and DAO data, we are looking to aggregate and create dashboards to give the best of the best insights to our community on an ongoing basis.

* Last week we had a kick off AMA session with Matt and Alex from RadicalxChange Foundation to go over the plan to [integrate RxC Voice into our Season 14 Budget process](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/harnessing-collective-intelligence-for-workstreams-with-radicalxchange-voice/10024). The dashboard is still being built out, so expect more metrics and visuals to come. The version 2 of the [Steward Report Cards](https://www.daostewards.xyz/) are also in the works with better metrics for engagement of our awesome Stewards. More on this will be shared in the coming weeks.
