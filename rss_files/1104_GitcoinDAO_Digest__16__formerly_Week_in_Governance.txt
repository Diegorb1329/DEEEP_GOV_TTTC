### [ü§ñ Meta-Governance](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/governance-meta/6)

* [The proposal to ratify the results of GR11](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmQTMLG8qbDpTCCUwVnbEmzLkdCvf1ApZ8w9nd9idfC8r7) has been ratified on Snapshot, the majority vote going towards allowing to pay all grants the amount of match funds from the community multisig as determined by applying QF after removing contributions attributed to accounts flagged as sybil.

* @Huxwell gave an [update](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-gitcoin-discovery-decentralize-the-knowledge-base-quests/8747/15) on the Discovery-workstream proposal after two weeks of discussion on the forum. ‚ÄúWe‚Äôd like to move forward by creating a vote on Snapshot in the following days that will contain an updated and simplified version for the proposal and budget request‚Äù. The updated proposal [Integrate dCompass within dGitcoin to build dQuests & dKnowledge](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-integrate-dcompass-within-dgitcoin-to-build-dquests-dknowledge/8836). The name of the workstream has been renamed from *Discovery* to **dCompass**.

* The Community Experience team spearheaded by @krrisis is making great progress on streamlining cross-DAO operations, onboarding flows, a Notion and Discord rework, and more. In this context ‚Äú[Gitcoin‚Äôs orbit](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoins-orbit/8812)‚Äù by Kevin Owocki explores the Gitcoin ecosystem and onboarding by drawing parallels to a solar system. The post also describes what a ‚ÄúDAO of DAOs‚Äù-solar system might look like.

* **[Renee Lee](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/lee0007)** started a [discussion](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/how-we-achieve-our-mission/8760/3) in the [How we achieve our mission](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/how-we-achieve-our-mission/8760)-thread on ‚Äúwhich role(s) the GitcoinDAO Ambassador may play within quadratic diplomacy and coordinating culture + capital‚Äù.

* @kelsien [raised questions and concerns](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/introducing-steward-report-cards/8712/14) regarding the Steward Report Cards and the metrics displayed on the cards. Kelsie links to a [recently published paper](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3907693#) that explores the relationship between on-chain transactions and surveillance.

## üå± [**Public Goods Funding**](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-funding/129)

* The public library continues to grow thanks to @linht.tran, Pan, and others, with speakers like Henry Zhu and Mat Dryhurst stopping by to talk about the web3 ethos.

* @annika, @jihad, @lanitrock, @Vizzbk and others have begun pushing forward on ops and fundraising for GR12 including for three themed rounds: climate, tech advocacy, and longevity.

* Lee and QZ are working with FDD on what a DAO Wayfinder role could look like.

* QZ and Rafa have independently begun thinking about what cross-DAO onboarding might look like alongside the Inter-DAO Alliance.

* Large & established organizations like the Gates Foundation are inquiring about supporting digital public goods and web3 tooling.

* [The Greatest LARP](http://www.greatestlarp.com) launched on Wednesday, October 20. Come defeat Moloch and fund public goods with us!

* Every Friday the Moonshot Collective gets together to show off their progress. ## üåê [Decentralize Gitcoin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-decentralize-gitcoin/180)

* The POC instance of [dGrants](https://grants.gtcdao.net), which is acting as our current production environment, now has 11 grants created by the Moonshot Collective team.

* Test data has been cleaned up and the round has been updated in preparation for the marketing push on Oct 20th at 3PM EST.

* A number of bugs have been fixed and a significant number of changes have been made based on feedback that we have received from internal testing.

* The budget proposal for Q4 2021 is coming soon along with the dCompass teams proposal for budget within the Decentralize Gitcoin workstream.

## üíô [Fraud Detection & Defense (FDD)](https://www.notion.so/gitcoin/Fraud-Detection-Defense-f1a2969a9f324f57a02ef69a60b25cae)

* [Proposal](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmQTMLG8qbDpTCCUwVnbEmzLkdCvf1ApZ8w9nd9idfC8r7) to ratify the results of Grants Round 11 passed. Payouts start streaming on 10/21/21. @DisruptionJoe authored a [Twitter thread](https://twitter.com/DisruptionJoe/status/1450090116366667777?s=20) on the topic of fraud tax and why it will not be paid out this round.

* Open sourced [DAO contributor agreements](https://github.com/LarryFlorio/LegalForms/commit/89b536591ea1ef897dfa87275a145ef2c8355f46) are being sent to all contributors.

* Contributor payments for Epochs 1-3 were sent out by FDD and Epochs 4-6 will be sent next week. Q4 payments will stream weekly or biweekly.

* The FDD-Budget Proposal for Q4 is about to be released. 90,000 GTC which includes one quarter of reserves for the defense streams are being requested. The [State of FDD](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uEuGe5OOecLyAsLbgnqaLplaH8ycU4zej3c-mv6QHTI/edit?usp=sharing) companion piece provides further details about the current role of the Workstream, which includes a Roadmap to Progressive Decentralization, how FDD provides value to GitcoinDAO, and a breakdown of all FDD initiatives.
