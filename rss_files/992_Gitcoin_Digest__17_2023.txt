## Passport

Checkout the new brand identity [here](https://www.figma.com/file/QvXFaL4bgPQgJuYXA9fN7b/Passport-Brand-Identity?type=design&node-id=1401-554&mode=design) üôÇ

## üìä Active Proposals:

* @garm proposes [Gitcoin to diversify a 10% of its concentrated stablecoin holdings into Glo Dollar](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-diversify-gitcoin-stablecoin-holdings-by-exchanging-usdc-for-glo-dollars/16398), a US-regulated stablecoin supporting basic income for ending extreme poverty, to enhance financial resilience, social impact, and promote a healthier crypto ecosystem.

* @rohit requests feedback on his [working prototype for Grantee Discovery Using LLM-Enabled Conversations](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gg18-grantee-discovery-using-llm-enabled-conversations/16192). The prototype allows users to ask questions, propose work descriptions, and seek assistance for GG participation. The tool is designed to maintain fairness, avoid biases, and not manipulate users' grantee preferences by focusing on enhancing interactions, memory capabilities, guiding users to reliable sources, and capturing feedback.
