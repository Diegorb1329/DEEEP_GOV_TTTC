# tldr

Gitcoin's [mission](https://gitcoin.co/mission) is to build & fund digital public goods (Open Source Software, Journalism, Protocol Research, etc) 

Gitcoin Grants Round 12 is our twelfth quarterly crowdfund matching campaign, designed to create contributions for these causes.

You can browse the grants at [https://gitcoin.co/grants](https://gitcoin.co/grants).

# wtf is qf?

Gitcoin Grants is built upon **Quadratic Funding (QF)**. 

[(well, a variant of it if you want to be technical about it)](https://ethresear.ch/t/pairwise-coordination-subsidies-a-new-quadratic-funding-design/5553)

Quadratic Funding is a math formula that dictates that we match crowdfunding contributions based upon the **number of unique contributors, NOT the amount they contributed**.

Its not as complicated as it sounds.   In plain english, QF IS powerful because it can mean that, for the most popular grants, a very small crowdfund contribution will be matched by a lot from the matching pool.

![slider|690x355](upload://yWikoR98YmjUHCp50w3WaZ9fnx0.gif)

TLDR - its a more democratic way of running a matching crowdfund.

Learn more about quadratic funding [here](https://wtfisqf.com/?grant=&grant=&grant=&grant=&match=1000).

# how much money is in the round

Round 12 Combined size of matching pools: >$2.5M

**Main Round**

- Main Round - $1M - Funding public goods, primarily in the Ethereum ecosystem

**Ecosystem Rounds**

- Uniswap - $50k - Supporting open source in Uniswap's ecosystem 
- Polygon - $100k - Supporting open source in Polygon's ecosystem 
- Forefront - $25k - Supporting open source in Forefront's ecosystem 
- ZKTech - $50k - Supporting open source in zk's ecosystem 
- Synthetix - $75k - Supporting open source in Synthetix's ecosystem 
- Gitcoin Building Gitcoin - $25k - Funding projects building Gitcoin's ecosystem 

**Cause Rounds**

- Climate Change - $250k - Funding projects combatting climate change 
- Longevity - $65k (an additional $150k to $1M up in the air)- Funding open science & longevity research 
- Advocacy - $900k - Funding projects that defend digital human rights
