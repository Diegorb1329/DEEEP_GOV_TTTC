https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/forum-guidelines-and-code-of-conduct-2022/758
    # Rules of Engagement :vulcan:

→ 1. In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we ask that you be respectful to each others thoughts and opinions. We will not tolerate any insults, racism, harassment or threats of violence against members, even as a joke. 

→ 2. This is a community space free from advertising. Do not post unsolicited referral links, or discord invitations unless specifically asked for by another user. 

→ 3. Do not post any NSFW content and limit the use of offensive language across all channels and nicknames.

→ 4. Never share API or secret keys, whether they are free or worthless. Those are for your use and your use only. 

→ 5. No user should ever ask you to send them money, especially not members of the moderation team. Please contact any available @admins immediately if that happens to you.

→ 6. It is an offense to impersonate members of the moderation team. Anyone caught doing this will be instantly banned and reported.

→ 7. As this is a free platform we cannot guarantee that the text communications in this server aren’t recorded and monitored by law enforcement. Please be conscious of this by refraining from discussing anything illegal including tax evasion and market manipulation.

→ 8. As you can see, this forum is organized by topics, please keep discussion in their respective threads or one of the moderators will assign it to the proper one for you, we want to keep discussion organized and clean, so please keep things relevant and help us encourage productive focused discussion. 

The full code of conduct can be found on our website here: https://support.gitcoin.co/gitcoin-policy/code-of-conduct

            <p>Chinese version<br>

社区规则：<br>
→ 1. 为了营造一个开放和热情的环境，我们要求您尊重彼此的想法和意见。我们不会容忍任何对会员的侮辱、种族主义、骚扰或暴力威胁，即使是开玩笑也不行。</p>
<p>→ 2. 这是一个没有广告的社区空间。除非其他用户特别要求，否则不要发布任何推荐链接或有异议的邀请。</p>
<p>→ 3. 不要发布任何 NSFW（不合适的内容或链接） 内容，在所有频道和昵称中限制使用攻击性语言。</p>
<p>→ 4. 永远不要共享 API 或密钥，无论它们是免费的还是毫无价值的。那些是仅供您使用的且仅限您使用。</p>
<p>→ 5. 任何用户都不应要求您向他们汇款，尤其是非审核团队的成员。如果您遇到这种情况，请立即使用@admins联系社区成员。</p>
<p>→ 6. 冒充审核团队成员是违法行为。任何被发现这样做的人都会被立即从社区驱逐并被报告。</p>
<p>→ 7. 由于这是一个免费平台，我们无法保证该服务器中的文本通信不会被执法部门记录和监控。请注意这一点，避免讨论任何非法行为，包括逃税和市场操纵。</p>
<p>→ 8. 如您所见，本论坛是按主题组织的，请在各自的主题中进行讨论，否则审核人员会将您的讨论的内容分配到合适主题，我们希望保持讨论的有序和干净，所以请保持内容相关并帮助我们鼓励富有成效的集中讨论。</p>
<p>完整的行为准则可以在我们的网站上找到：<a href="https://support.gitcoin.co/kb/article/42-code-of-conduct/" rel="noopener nofollow ugc">行为准则-gitcoin-由HappyFox 58提供</a></p>
