* Catch all the details in the post by [Kris](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/krrisis/summary) for [Gitcoin Grants Citizens Rounds](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/rewarding-the-community-gitcoin-citizen-round-1/14905) to reward people and grassroots projects that have contributed to Gitcoin‚Äôs success. With ‚ÄåMatching Pool funding of 13.3K GTC (20K DAI), ‚Äåapplications for the Citizen Round will officially open on Monday May 15.
* In the post [Gitcoin: who makes the decisions anyway?](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-who-makes-the-decisions-anyway/14537), [Shawn](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/shawn16400/summary) seeks input on identifying hot spots in the use of Approval Matrix and DACI models that define the lifecycle of how the decision gets from identification to approval.
* [David](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/davidacasey/summary) shares the proposal for Gitcoin to [Support the Funding the Commons Hackathon, Residency and Conference Series](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-support-the-funding-the-commons-hackathon-residency-and-conference-series/14904). The event series would directly utilize Gitcoin‚Äôs Grants Stack in a variety of ways, including to deploy funding to projects by running a Funding the Commons grant round.
* [Eugene](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/eleventh19/summary) outlines the proposal to produce a report on the [current state of web3 grants](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-state-of-web3-grants-report/14562). Besides being a market analysis work for Grants Stack, the insights from the report would serve as a public good for entire ecosystem to learn and benefit from.

## **üß≠** **Weekly Workstream Update**

Here are key highlights from the [weekly workstream update](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1WJseBI_raaBPhZFxTD-ProPvb9e9Ay-6vzWSERKmgmY/edit?usp=sharing) (video [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J0ckCHlK6Z8)):

**Allo + Grants Stack:**

* Current focus ([roadmap here](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVPsTabFA=/?share_link_id=770500701816)): Improvements and fixes for Beta feedback; Solarpunk - Round edit flow; Lunarpunk - Post round-UI for Linear QF
* Upcoming efforts will include enhancing the QF experience, quadratic voting, and utilizing the [Productboard](https://www.productboard.com/) tool for roadmaps to bring more visibility to the backlog
* REMINDER! Support has moved over to Intercom (widget available in the bottom right-hand corner in the apps). Users should report product-related bugs, suggestions, and questions in Intercom.
* [Template for Allo Improvement Process](https://github.com/allo-protocol/aips/pull/7) is now available to utilize community feedback for Allo
* Documentation and tutorials in progress for voting and payout strategy with documentation for project registry integration lined up next
* Upcoming Allo [integrations](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Allo-Integrations-115d3f4aebbe432fa616221c1716b0ff) include Privy Sweepstakes (expected May 22), Endaoment (confirmed, dev started), Decent (dev starts soon)
* Q2 priorities for DevRel can be reviewed [here](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Q2-2023-Dev-Ecosystem-Priorities-151d258304fb4173bee5a095d59b6947)
* [Allo Brand Moodboards](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1k4xnL8GUtLUl0q8ycjLcXlbHqwOTtGDz/view?pli=1) reviewed and approved; focus on logo concepts and typography selection
* Grants Stack Self Serve marketing plan and moodboards are in progress

**Public Goods Funding**

* View the [metrics dashboard](https://gitcoin-grants.streamlit.app/) to see contributions across rounds and projects for Gitcoin Beta Round. Approximately, a total of 107K contributions have raised $600k in funding. Most rounds witnessed a 1.5x to 3x jump in contributions just in the last week of the round.
* 117K instances of the [Stand with Crypto](https://twitter.com/coinbase/status/1650612986980048897?s=20) NFT minted
* Post round focus on conducting Sybil analysis and fraud detection before the final calculations

**Passport**

* New stamp available for [guild.xyz](https://guild.xyz/); focus on bug fixes and copy updates based on Beta Round feedback, along with onchain stamps (EAS)
* Roadmap updates for introduction of new stamps (e.g. Hypercert, Civic, Holonym) in progress
* Upcoming focus on data dashboard, Unique Humanity Score improvements, UX improvements, Ceramic ComposeDB Upgrade
* [Market Lanscape Analysis](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Gitcoin-Passport-Market-Landscape-Analysis-May-2023-e1dcab1d89004fbc8077bebecf7acf27) presented to Passport Pod; review weekly release notes [here](https://gitcoin.notion.site/What-s-New-Gitcoin-Passport-4764ed4f8a3f42fdbd2d5d7dd8637872)
* Sample library completed; winner identified for [‚Äåbounty](https://daoglobalhackathon.com/bounties) at DAO Global Hackathon hosted by Aragon
* Integration in progress with Snapshot for migrating to API; technical guide for Score integration

**MMM**

* As part of efforts to reposition Gitcoin as a tech-first organization, the [Gitcoin Thought Leadership Program](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17bt-jnXc1jEdWGJdBl97XksZtiMx4LF5AvzP0CVsimA/edit?usp=sharing) will help existing and emerging thought leaders share their expertise to build eminence
* Pages in progress: Allo page, Program page, Grants Stack v2 page, revised home page, and a refresh for WTFisQF, search and topic sections for Blog, media center ([full schedule](https://sharing.clickup.com/42083557/l/h/6-900900854532-1/0a09648fad87ae5))
* In progress: Grants Stack visual identity, Canva template, [Impact Report and Research](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-state-of-web3-grants-report/14562), DAO-wide visual audit, Metalabel round 2
* Roles open for [publicist](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1qRL2ccPQrs5trjPf-nhG-fAgK-cPfZM-CrF1rA75k3c/edit) and [designers](https://wellfound.com/company/gitcoin/jobs)
* Ratification upcoming shortly for DAO [Mission, Vision, Brand values](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/discussion-poll-aligning-on-gitcoins-mission-vision/13639)
