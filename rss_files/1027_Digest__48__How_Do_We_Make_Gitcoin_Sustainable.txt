## How Do We Create Sustainable Revenue Streams to Fund the DAO?

This week, we explored the question ‚Äú[How does a DAO become sustainable?](https://twitter.com/gitcoin/status/1610002349644210179?s=20&t=-r_K4qIlg4DsEiJUZAD1jA)‚Äù in our Gitcoin Community Call on Wednesday. More specifically, we asked, ‚ÄúHow does Gitcoin become sustainable?‚Äù.

One of the main questions during the call was, ‚Äòshould we design grants protocols to take a small fee on every grants donation?‚Äô One community member noted that ‚ÄúGofundme charges 2.9% +$0.30 on every donation‚Äù.

Others on the call raised concerns that the grants protocol was more likely to be forked if we charged a transaction fee. Ben West offered his perspective that, ‚ÄúI think the forking risk is lower than we might think. Most of our partners don't have the staff time and expertise to run their own rounds and will want/need our support‚Äù.

During the call, we jammed in a Miro board to crowdsource [potential answers to these questions here](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVP1yGiAY=/?share_link_id=554160644229).

*This is a topic that we will continue to discuss in the coming year! Check the [#dao-citizens channel](https://discord.com/channels/562828676480237578/876873641453826100) for the recording of the call.*
