### [ü§ñ Meta-Governance](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/governance-meta/6)

* The first on-chain vote for paying out GTC to GR10 "Building Gitcoin" participants was put live by @austingriffith. More information [here](https://twitter.com/austingriffith/status/1426258712503615488)

* An initial [DAO Onboarding Typeform](https://form.typeform.com/to/Q2twNqCD) was put together by @DisruptionJoe and @ceresstation to help new participants get engaged.

* The Gitcoin Grantee Content Creation + Storytelling-Workstream proposal by @thealexyao has been [updated](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-proposal-gitcoin-grantee-content-creation-storytelling/8070/10) after feedback from the community. @ken and @Pop are continuing to provide guidance to iterate towards a version to be put to vote.

* @owocki authored a thread on how [Gitcoin/GitcoinDAO Egregore is Emerging](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/the-gitcoin-gitcoindao-egregore-is-emerging/8200). The post is ‚Äúdesigned to provide some food for thought on Gitcoin/GitcoinDAO - where it‚Äôs been, where it is not, and where it‚Äôs going.‚Äù

* The first [Gitcoin Stewards Unconference Chat](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/gitcoin-stewards-unconference-chats/8112) by @Anett will be held on [Aug 18th](https://www.addevent.com/event/ke8057713) and continue on a monthly basis.

* @Developer-piyush posted a RFP on [GitcoinDAO for climate change](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-gitcoindao-for-climate-change/8166) and proposed the formation of a Climate DAO.

### [üßô‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏èüßô‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è Workstream Discussion](https://gov.gitcoin.co/c/workstream-discussion/9)

[Public Goods Funding / Treasury Management](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-funding/129)

* The public goods discussion group held its [second meeting](https://twitter.com/futurealisha/status/1425943393855627267) to help onboard new contributors and get more people thinking about public goods in the context of @ceresstation  and @Toby's  [call to action](https://gitcoin.co/blog/seeking-a-new-kind-of-public-good/).
* The first [workstream budget](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmQWucgbue8uemNHXTDKvvEhRxfEcoB6B6Ci51TANkKaGU) by @Yalor has passed and is waiting to be put on Tally for on-chain ratification.

[Public Goods Prototyping (Moonshot Collective)](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-public-goods-prototyping/130)

* The [budget proposal](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/prototyping-workstream-moonshot-budget-request/8074) put forward by @austingriffith and the Moonshot Collective workstream for Q3 2021 [passed](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmSYVGy8KMrWu3Lrue8ah9zrWZb4wrCJboi4o5KDmNS6P2) and action has been taken to push forward on a formal on-chain vote.

[Decentralization Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-decentralize-gitcoin/180)

* The [vote](https://snapshot.org/#/gitcoindao.eth/proposal/QmchtUum2drB3NAgCqDxKWVhqs8JrCdGHX5W5s4CHsVMw1) for the Decentralize Gitcoin Workstream [budget](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/decentralize-gitcoin-workstream-budget-request/8121) is live with a deadline on Aug 17th.
* The requests made by @owocki to decentralize [Gitcoin‚Äôs hosting](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-gitcoindao-com-website/8114/),[ Gitcoin Quests](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-decentralize-gitcoin-quests/8120),[ Gitcoin Kudos](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-decentralize-gitcoin-kudos/8115),[ Gitcoin Avatar Builder](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-decentralized-gitcoin-avatar-builder/8117) and[ Gitcoin Profiles](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/request-for-proposal-decentralize-gitcoin-profiles/8116) has received multiple proposals from the community.

[Fraud Detection & Defense (FDD-WG)](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-fraud-detection-and-defense-working-group-assemble/158) former Anti-Sybil/Anti-Fraud

* A [budget request proposal](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/proposal-fdd-wg-anti-fraud-sybil-collusion-q3-budget-request/8210) to fund the FDD-WG workstream for Q3 2021 was posted by @DisruptionJoe.
* The workstream is ramping up for GR11 to ensure robust fraud detection

[Merch, Memes, Marketing Workstream](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-suggestion-mmm-merch-memes-marketing-for-public-goods-gitcoin-gitcoindao/7066)

MMM is currently working on a number of starting projects:

* A new public goods merch website
* Steward cards to help the community understand
* Map of the quadratic lands
* Onboarding new team members and increasing Meme VelocityTM

That‚Äôs it for this week in Gitcoin DAO governance, feel free join the discussions via the links and on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/gitcoin)!
