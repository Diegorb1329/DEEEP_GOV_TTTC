* In the post [Trust & Reputation at Gitcoin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/trust-reputation-at-gitcoin/14125), [Alex](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/ale.k/summary) identified three key threats and forward looking measures to grow Gitcoin‚Äôs reputation and community trust: security vulnerabilities, over-generosity with the "in-crowd," and obscured transparency in decision-making
* [Alexa](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/alexalombardo/summary) invited feedback on the [Future of Schelling Point](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/discussion-feedback-request-future-of-schelling-point/14259), including the possibility of decentralizing it further. The post does a recap of the strategic marketing role Schelling Point serves for Gitcoin and our products, plus its role in the broader ecosystem.

## **üß≠** **Weekly Workstream Update**

Here are key highlights from the [weekly workstream update](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1WJseBI_raaBPhZFxTD-ProPvb9e9Ay-6vzWSERKmgmY/edit?usp=sharing) (video [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oZY3eKtgldw)):

**Allo + Grants Stack:**

* Exceeded the goal of running 13 quadratic funding rounds (see all of the 15 Gitcoin Rounds and Featured Rounds [here](https://grants.gitcoin.co/)). 2 opportunities in sight each for Quadratic Voting and Direct Grants programs.
* Improvements and fixes for Explorer Landing Page, performance optimization (CDN implementation, image size updates), approval issues due to Metamask bug.
* Feedback addressed from Alpha Rounds: Rich text, project socials validation, project preview, etc.)
* Beta round feedback: High gas fees, single cart experience, matching estimates, seamless transition from creating a project to applying to a round, clarity in reasons for rejection of a project
* First Allo integration launched with [Charmverse](https://twitter.com/CharmVerse/status/1651303937679425543) - All grant applicants get a claimable space for project management to onboard & engage their community
* Upcoming [integrations](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Allo-Integrations-115d3f4aebbe432fa616221c1716b0ff) include Privy Sweepstakes (Expected May 22), Endaoment (confirmed, dev started), Decent (dev starts soon)
* Initiated [supporting documentation](https://gitcoin.notion.site/Allo-Technical-Docs-36dc1ef6c7f04a1ba4185ac652f32659) with first updates out on May 12
* Final Allo brand direction to be shared this week followed by initial logo concepts; Allo v1 soft launch is May 15
* Program manager education hub and content planning in progress for [Self Serve Launch](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1TP0Y91JiCX2S9TlOVf8ojJ8t8ja9RqFMbLFb-x3wGxE/edit#slide=id.g209e08390e7_0_0); app redesigns are in progress
* Current focus ([roadmap here](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVPsTabFA=/?share_link_id=770500701816)): Solarpunk - Round edit flow; Lunarpunk - Post round-UI for Linear QF

**Public Goods Funding**

* View the [metrics dashboard](https://gitcoin-grants.streamlit.app/) to see contributions across rounds and projects for the Gitcoin Beta Round. Approximately 44K contributions have raised $275k as of May 3rd.
* Also, 99.8K instances of the [Stand with Crypto](https://twitter.com/coinbase/status/1650612986980048897?s=20) NFT minted so far - Zora will be contributing funds from the mint to the Advocacy Round and future rounds

**Passport**

* Focus on bug fixes and copy updates based on Beta Round feedback, along with onchain stamps
* Roadmap updates for introduction of new stamps in progress
* Onboarded DevRel engineer, sample library in progress with focus on integration guides next
* [Quick Start video guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bfkalDSvRDc) for the Scorer App has 47k views; 8000+ new followers on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/gitcoinpassport); [Gitcoin Passport Guild](https://guild.xyz/gitcoinpassport) has 3000+ members
* Ongoing focus on competitive research, sharing weekly [release notes](https://gitcoin.notion.site/What-s-New-Gitcoin-Passport-4764ed4f8a3f42fdbd2d5d7dd8637872)
* Some data nuggets on Passport - 162k GTC staked @ $280k USD value ATH; compared to apx 31K passports created during Alpha, 75K passports have been created so far in Beta Round; number of tickets so far in Beta are 30% relative to Alpha

**MMM**

* The next version of the website update will focus on Allo page, Program page, Grants Stack v2 page, revised Home page, and a refresh for WTFisQF ([full schedule](https://sharing.clickup.com/42083557/l/h/6-900900854532-1/0a09648fad87ae5))
* Things to look out for: Long-term brand / product architecture, DAO-wide visual audit, DAO thought leadership strategy, future of Schelling Point
