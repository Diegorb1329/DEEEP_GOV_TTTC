***After a brief hiatus, we are excited to bring back the Digest - a go-to source for all the latest news, updates, and highlights about Gitcoin DAO.***

### üî• Hot Topics:

* **Changes to budget proposal process:** [Kris](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/krrisis/summary) shares details of the passed proposal for amendments to the [workstream budget proposal process](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/workstream-budget-proposal-process-v3/13838/1) for Gitcoin, including moving to a 6-month budget cycle with the aim of improving and standardizing the process.

* **Update on Beta Round eligibility:** [Kieran](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/koday/summary) shares a[ followup for the Gitcoin Program Beta Round Eligibility](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/follow-up-gitcoin-program-beta-round-eligibility/13837) policy. This is a follow-up to a previous [forum post](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/discussion-feedback-request-gitcoin-program-beta-round-eligibility/13306) and references the most debated topics including quid pro quo, multiple projects per person/org, multiple rounds/matching pools per grant, and prior external funding.

* **FDD Workstream Dissolution:** [DisruptionJoe](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/disruptionjoe/summary) offers details on the [FDD Workstream Dissolution](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/fdd-workstream-dissolution-details/13852) and the transition to having fraud defense a vital part of end-to-end accountable product workstreams. FDD will host a community call on April 26th to do a public retro and celebration of this workstream‚Äôs work.

* **Update on Flexible Voting Governer:** In an update for the [upgrade to the Gitcoin Governor Contracts](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/feedback-upgrading-the-gitcoin-governor-contracts-flexible-voting/12204/22), [Kyle](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kyle/summary) and [Ben](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/bendi/summary) share that the audit of the Flexible Voting Governor with OpenZeppelin has been completed and the [results](https://blog.openzeppelin.com/scopelift-flexible-voting-audit/) are available for public review.

* **Gitcoin sponsorship guidelines:** [CoachJ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/coachjonathan/summary) has created an advice-seeking post for [Gitcoin Sponsorship Guidelines](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/discussion-gitcoin-sponsorship-guidelines/13798). The post discusses the definition of sponsorship, recent examples of potential sponsorships, and roles and responsibilities for decision-making.

Also, here are hot topics to catch-up on from earlier this year in case you missed a few!

* **Overview of Gitcoin Grants Stack and Allo Protocol:** [Nate](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/nategosselin/summary), [Laura](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/viriya/summary), [Alexa](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/alexalombardo/summary) have co-authored an overview post for [Gitcoin Grants Stack & Allo Protocol](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/introducing-gitcoin-grants-stack-allo-protocol-product-overviews-part-1-of-2/12664). This is foundational reading for anyone learning about the two products and Gitcoin‚Äôs future as a protocol DAO.

* **Aligning on Gitcoin‚Äôs Mission & Vision:** [Alexa](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/alexalombardo/summary) shares a recap and a poll for aligning on [Gitcoin‚Äôs Mission & Vision](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/discussion-poll-aligning-on-gitcoins-mission-vision/13639). The statements were proposed earlier to inform the brief for our brand visual refresh and will appear on the new Gitcoin website and other marketing or partnerships materials.

* **Refactoring Gitcoin: What falls between the cracks?** [Kevin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kevin.olsen/summary) identified and sought input on [two areas of coordination that required attention](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/refactoring-gitcoin-what-falls-between-the-cracks/13026). The first is the lack of low barrier onramps for bottom-up ideation and participation. The second topic in Kevin‚Äôs post highlights the inability to execute on narrower strategic areas that are deeply consequential but non-workstream specific tasks

* **Gitcoin‚Äôs progress and future:** [Kyle‚Äôs](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kyle/summary) post on [Gitcoin‚Äôs Progress and Future](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/march-2023-gitcoins-progress-and-future/13527) seeks inspiration from Vitalik's [roadmap for Ethereum](https://itsa-global.medium.com/the-future-of-ethereum-the-merge-surge-verge-purge-and-splurge-e1b9d043709e) and builds upon the DAO‚Äôs [Purpose and Essential Intents (EIs)](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/update-gitcoindaos-adopted-purpose-and-essential-intents/10984) defined last year. The post reviews the most important priorities, including the launch and success metrics for Allo Protocol, the Gitcoin Grants Program, and Gitcoin Passport.
