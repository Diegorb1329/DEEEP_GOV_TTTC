#### **1. ETH Denver (Feb 23 - Mar 1)**

#### **2. Financial & Treasury Update**

* **Treasury decline due to token price drop**:
  * **Dec 2024**: $20M (GTC @ $1.10)
  * **Feb 2025**: ~$9.8M (GTC @ $0.42)
* **Cost reductions in place** but a focus on revenue is critical.
* **Spending Plans**:
  * GL H2 Funding: -$1.5M
  * Allo Funding 2025: -$2M
  * **EOY Treasury = $6.3M**

---

#### **3. Revenue Growth Strategy**

* **2025 Revenue Target**: $2M (Make-or-break goal)
* **Key Focus**:
  * Large deals & big customers.
  * Token utility experiments for GTC.
  * Optimizing non-stablecoin revenue.
* **January Revenue Performance**:
  * **Polygon deal**: $90K revenue.
  * **Filecoin fees**: $30K revenue.
  * **Total January Revenue**: $120K (12% of H1 goal).
* **Q1 Pipeline**:
  * Several smaller deals ($10K-$20K each).
  * A **large potential deal ($3M GMV @ 10% fee).**
  * **Total pipeline value: $397K.**

---

#### **4. Gitcoin Grants (GG23) ‚Äì April 2-23**

* **Funding Rounds**:
  * **Quadratic Funding (QF)**:
    * $600K in matching.
    * Open to all Web3 projects.
  * **Retroactive Funding**:
    * Top 30 OSS projects from past Gitcoin Grants rounds.
    * $600K matching.
  * **Community Rounds**:
    * New governance model using Grant Ships.
    * Gamified incentives & on-chain success tracking.
