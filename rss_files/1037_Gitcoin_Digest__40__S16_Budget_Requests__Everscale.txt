# üî• Hot Topics
A roundup of interesting topics of the past week from within the GitcoinDAO ecosystem.

## ‚òëÔ∏è From the Governance Forum

* **[Breakdown of Round Manager Personas for the Protocol](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/breakdown-of-round-manager-personas-for-the-protocol/11775), by [Disruption Joe](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/DisruptionJoe)**

  Joe created this post detailing the Round Manager Personas in a table. They are: Match Pool Funder(lead), Match Pool Funder(minor), Ecosystem Owner, Round Operator(community-led), Round Operator(Gitcoin-led). Joe invites forum readers to comment/reply to the post, or to [**check out the Miro board here**](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVPJc88tc=/?share_link_id=755374366478).

* **Season 16 Workstream Budget Requests**

  Here‚Äôs a list of budget requests from GitcoinDAO‚Äôs five existing workstreams:

  [[S16 Proposal] DRAFT Gitcoin Product Collective Budget Request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s16-proposal-draft-gitcoin-product-collective-budget-request/11814), by [Kevin Olsen
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/kevin.olsen)[[S16 Proposal] DRAFT DAO Operations Budget Request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s16-proposal-draft-dao-operations-budget-request/11811), by [Jodi
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/Jodi_GitcoinDAO) [[S16 Proposal] DRAFT FDD Budget Request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s16-proposal-draft-fdd-budget-request/11821), by [tigress
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/tigress)[[S16 Proposal] DRAFT Public Goods Funding Budget Request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s16-proposal-draft-public-goods-funding-budget-request/11819), by [ceresstation
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/ceresstation)[[S16 Proposal] MMM Budget Request](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/s16-proposal-mmm-budget-request/11782), by [CoachJ](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/CoachJonathan)

* **[The New Grants Protocol Flywheel](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/the-new-grants-protocol-flywheel/11769) by [Disruption Joe](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/DisruptionJoe)**

  Joe builds on Owocki‚Äôs original ‚Äú[The Grants 2.0 Flywheel](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/the-grants-2-0-flywheel/10711)‚Äù post. The central question of the post is, ‚ÄúHow do we create sustainability in the mechanism?‚Äù(read Joe‚Äôs previous post [‚ÄúA model for Gitcoin sustainability‚Äù here](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/a-model-for-gitcoin-sustainability/10757)). Joe posits that, as we begin to roll out alpha and beta versions of grants protocols in the next 2-6 months, we can start onboarding other web3 ecosystems and communities now‚Äìin fact, the success of Grants 2.0 may depend on how well we onboard new communities to the grants protocol. He also talks about how GTC price and utility factor into this flywheel. Joe ends the post with a message that we, as a DAO, need to think deeply about sustainability, and which levers create the most impact.

* **[Messari Governor Note: Decentralizing Gitcoin](https://gov.gitcoin.co/t/messari-governor-note-decentralizing-gitcoin/11776) by [joel.miya
](https://gov.gitcoin.co/u/joel.miya)**
[Joel Miyazawa](https://twitter.com/Ichiro_JJ), an analyst at [Messari](https://messari.io/), wrote this report as an analysis on ‚Äú[the GR16 Structure proposal forum post] and Gitcoin‚Äôs transition to a decentralized protocol‚Äù. It‚Äôs [free to read on Messari‚Äôs site](https://messari.io/article/governor-note-decentralizing-gitcoin?utm_source=gov_gitcoin&utm_medium=organic_social&utm_campaign=decentralizing_gitcoin), and provides a good overview of Gitcoin‚Äôs transition to Grants 2.0 from an external perspective. Thank you, Joel!

## üéôLatest Posts from the Gitcoin Blog

* [**Announcing the decentralized future of Gitcoin Grants**](https://go.gitcoin.co/blog/announcing-the-decentralized-future-of-gitcoin-grants), by **Team Gitcoin**

  This post gives insight into Gitcoin‚Äôs process of decentralizing it‚Äôs grants program from a centralized platform, to a set of open source, decentralized protocols. Testing and soliciting feedback from previous Grantees is a top priority as we continue to roll-out the new grants protocols.

* [**Announcing: The Fantom Alpha Round**](https://go.gitcoin.co/blog/announcing-the-fantom-alpha-round), by **Team Gitcoin**

  The Fantom Foundation is leveraging Gitcoin‚Äôs grants protocol for an initial round allocating 125,000 FTM to projects building within the Fantom ecosystem. Through the partnership, Fantom builders will be able to use the protocol to secure matched funding that helps sustain and grow their projects.

## üéôLatest Episodes from GreenPill

* [GreenPill #58: What Information Wants with Rhys Lindmark](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jY_Tye7oIWc&t=1s) ‚Äì On this episode of Green Pill, Kevin is joined by [Rhys Lindmark](https://twitter.com/RhysLindmark), author of, ‚Äú[What Information Wants](https://rhyslindmark.substack.com/p/80-what-information-wants)‚Äù. The two dive into what, in fact, information actually wants, the fascinating ways in which information replicates, how we avoid a ‚Äòrace to the bottom‚Äô type scenario when it comes to information, and so much more.

* [GreenPill #59: Impact Networks with David Ehrlichman](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hDiBq0QO23U) ‚Äì [David Erlichman](https://twitter.com/davehrlichman) is author of the book, [Impact Networks: Create Connection, Spark Collaboration, and Catalyze Systemic Change](https://www.converge.net/book). He is also the co-founder of [Hats Protocol](https://twitter.com/hatsprotocol), which tokenizes contributor roles for DAOs. The study of networks is increasingly popular as an approach to understanding complex networks. David has combined theory and action through his writing and building to uncover patterns in how we organize and coordinate.
